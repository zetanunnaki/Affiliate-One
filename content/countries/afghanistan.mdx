## Server Coverage & Speed Performance

No major VPN provider operates physical servers inside Afghanistan, so users connect to nearby locations in Pakistan, India, or the United Arab Emirates. Expect baseline speeds of 5-15 Mbps on 4G connections in major cities, with VPN overhead reducing throughput by 15-30%. Connecting to servers in Dubai or Mumbai typically provides the lowest latency at 60-100ms, sufficient for encrypted messaging and basic web browsing. During peak congestion or government-ordered throttling events, speeds may drop significantly regardless of VPN usage.

## Best VPN Protocols for Afghanistan

Given active VPN blocking by Afghan ISPs, obfuscated protocols are critical. Standard OpenVPN and WireGuard connections are increasingly detected and dropped via deep packet inspection. NordVPN's obfuscated servers, Surfshark's Camouflage Mode, and Proton VPN's Stealth protocol all disguise VPN traffic as regular HTTPS, making them far more reliable. Users should pre-configure multiple protocol options before entering the country and keep manual connection files as backup in case apps cannot update.
