## Afghanistan: VPN Guide

Afghanistan's internet is restricted under Taliban governance. Social media and content blocking. VPN essential for accessing blocked services. Security situation significantly affects connectivity.

## Why Use a VPN in Afghanistan

- **Public Wi-Fi protection** — Encrypt traffic on shared networks
- **ISP privacy** — Prevent monitoring of your browsing activity
- **Content access** — Access home-country services while in Afghanistan
- **Communication security** — Protect messaging and VoIP calls
