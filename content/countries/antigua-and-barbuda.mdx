## Internet Freedom & Privacy Landscape

Antigua and Barbuda has not been assessed in Freedom House's Freedom on the Net report, but as a stable Caribbean parliamentary democracy, the twin-island nation maintains an open and unrestricted internet environment. The primary ISPs are Flow (owned by Liberty Latin America) and Digicel, providing 4G LTE mobile coverage and broadband services across both islands. Internet penetration exceeds 75%, with St. John's, English Harbour, and Jolly Harbour areas well-served by both mobile and fixed broadband. The country's growing reputation as a digital nomad destination and yacht charter hub drives demand for reliable, secure connectivity.

## VPN Legality

VPN use is fully legal in Antigua and Barbuda. There are no laws restricting VPN technology or encrypted communications. Both residents and visitors can use VPN services freely for any lawful purpose, including privacy protection and accessing geo-restricted content.

## Server Coverage & Speed Performance

No major VPN provider operates servers directly in Antigua and Barbuda. The nearest server locations are in the US (Miami, approximately 2,500 km away) and other major Caribbean hubs. Users can expect latency of 45-70ms to Miami servers via the submarine cable infrastructure connecting the Eastern Caribbean. This delivers performance suitable for HD streaming, video conferencing, and general browsing without noticeable lag. NordVPN maintains over 100 servers in the Miami area, while Surfshark offers strong US East Coast coverage. The islands' connection to the Eastern Caribbean Fiber System ensures stable international bandwidth.

## Best VPN Protocols for Antigua and Barbuda

WireGuard is the optimal protocol for Antigua and Barbuda, maximizing speed on Flow and Digicel's networks while minimizing latency additions on Caribbean submarine cable routes. IKEv2 provides a strong alternative for mobile users, reconnecting seamlessly when transitioning between cell towers while island-hopping between Antigua and Barbuda. Since the country does not implement any form of VPN blocking, throttling, or deep packet inspection, obfuscation features are unnecessary and users should prioritize speed-focused configurations.
