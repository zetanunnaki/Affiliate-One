## Armenia: VPN Guide

Armenia has good internet in Yerevan with a growing IT hub. No significant internet restrictions. The tech sector is one of the fastest-growing in the Caucasus.

## Why Use a VPN in Armenia

- **Public Wi-Fi protection** — Encrypt traffic on shared networks
- **ISP privacy** — Prevent monitoring of your browsing activity
- **Content access** — Access home-country services while in Armenia
- **Communication security** — Protect messaging and VoIP calls
