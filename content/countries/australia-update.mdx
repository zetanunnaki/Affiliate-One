## Australia 2026 Update: NBN Improvements

Australia's NBN (National Broadband Network) has improved significantly:
- **FTTP upgrade program:** Millions of homes upgrading from FTTN to full fiber (FTTP)
- **Speed tiers:** Up to 1 Gbps now available on FTTP connections
- **Fixed Wireless:** Improved for regional areas using 5G technology
- **Starlink:** Popular in remote and rural areas where NBN doesn't reach

## Data Retention: Still 2 Years

Australia's metadata retention law remains unchanged — ISPs must store your connection metadata for 2 years:
- Who you communicated with, when, and how
- 80+ government agencies can access without a warrant
- A VPN prevents your ISP from associating this metadata with your browsing activity
- Five Eyes membership means Australian intelligence shares data with US, UK, Canada, NZ

## Remote Work Down Under

Australia's remote work culture is well-established:
- **Hybrid work:** Standard for most office jobs in major cities
- **Regional hubs:** Government encouraging remote work in regional towns
- **Café culture:** Strong in Sydney, Melbourne, Brisbane — all with free Wi-Fi
- **Co-working:** WeWork, Hub Australia, Spaces in every major city

## 2026 Testing Update

- **NordVPN** achieves 800 Mbps on Sydney servers — significant improvement with NBN upgrades
- **Surfshark** best value for Australian families with unlimited devices
- **FastestVPN** most consistent across Australia's vast distances including Perth and Darwin
