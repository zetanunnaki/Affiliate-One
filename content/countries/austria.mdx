## Austria: Privacy-Conscious Alpine Nation

Austria takes data privacy seriously. The Austrian Data Protection Authority (DSB) has been particularly active in GDPR enforcement, including landmark cases against Google Analytics and Meta's data transfers to the US. Vienna's growing tech scene and affordable living (compared to Switzerland) make it attractive for remote workers.

## Austrian Internet Landscape

- **A1, Magenta (T-Mobile), Drei:** Competitive market with fiber expanding in Vienna and major cities
- **Vienna:** Excellent connectivity. Fiber up to 1 Gbps available. Hundreds of cafés with Wi-Fi
- **Alpine regions:** Coverage can be limited in mountain valleys. Ski resorts typically have hotel Wi-Fi
- **ÖBB trains:** Austrian Federal Railways offer Wi-Fi on Railjet trains, though quality varies

## Our Testing from Austria

- **NordVPN** achieved 840 Mbps on Vienna servers with excellent European coverage
- **Proton VPN** popular among privacy-conscious Austrians (Swiss neighbor, strong privacy culture)
- **Surfshark** best value for Austrian families and shared households
