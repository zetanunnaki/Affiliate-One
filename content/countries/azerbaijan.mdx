## Azerbaijan: Oil Wealth With Internet Controls

Azerbaijan has invested in broadband infrastructure, particularly in Baku, but the government monitors internet traffic and has blocked some content. Social media has been restricted during political events, and some VPN services have been blocked.

## What's Restricted

- **Social media:** Facebook, Instagram, and messaging apps have been restricted during protests and political events
- **VPN services:** Some VPN websites and apps blocked. Premium services generally still work
- **News sites:** Independent and opposition media periodically blocked
- **Surveillance:** Government monitors internet traffic through ISP-level controls

## Baku Digital Scene

- **Azercell, Bakcell, Nar:** Major mobile carriers with good Baku coverage
- **Broadband:** Fiber expanding in central Baku. 50-200 Mbps available
- **Co-working:** Growing in Baku as the tech sector develops
- **Formula 1 circuit:** Baku's F1 infrastructure brought improved telecoms
