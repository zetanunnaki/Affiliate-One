## Bangladesh: Growing Internet, Growing Restrictions

Bangladesh has a rapidly growing internet user base — over 130 million people online, primarily through mobile data. However, the government has imposed internet shutdowns during political protests and blocked social media platforms, making VPN use increasingly common and important.

## Internet Shutdowns

Bangladesh has a history of government-imposed internet blackouts:
- **2024 protests:** Extended internet shutdown during student-led protests, one of the longest in the country's history
- **Social media blocks:** Facebook, messaging apps periodically restricted during political events
- **VPN surge:** Each shutdown drives massive VPN adoption. Bangladesh consistently ranks among top VPN-downloading countries during political crises

## Mobile-First Market

Like India, Bangladesh is overwhelmingly mobile-first:
- **Grameenphone, Robi, Banglalink:** Major carriers with growing 4G coverage
- **bKash and Nagad:** Mobile money dominates financial transactions
- **Affordable data:** Mobile data prices are among the lowest in the region
- **Fixed broadband:** Limited to major cities, primarily Dhaka and Chittagong
