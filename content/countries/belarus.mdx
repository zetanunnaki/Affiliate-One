## Belarus: VPN Guide

Belarus has periodically blocked VPN services and restricted internet during political unrest. Government monitors internet extensively. Configure VPN before entering the country.

## Why Use a VPN in Belarus

- **Public Wi-Fi protection** — Encrypt traffic on shared networks
- **ISP privacy** — Prevent monitoring of your browsing activity
- **Content access** — Access home-country services while in Belarus
- **Communication security** — Protect messaging and VoIP calls
