## Bolivia: VPN Guide

Bolivia has growing internet in La Paz, Cochabamba, and Santa Cruz. Altitude and remote areas affect coverage. No significant internet restrictions.

- **Content access** — Access home-country services while abroad
- **Remote work security** — Protect work data on any network
