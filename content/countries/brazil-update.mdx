## Brazil 2026 Update: LGPD Gets Real

Brazil's LGPD (data protection law) enforcement has ramped up significantly. The ANPD (National Data Protection Authority) has issued its first major fines and is becoming a credible privacy regulator. This increases privacy awareness but doesn't eliminate the need for personal VPN protection.

## Pix and Financial Security

Brazil's Pix instant payment system now processes over 4 billion transactions per month:
- **Pix fraud:** Remains a significant concern. Criminals use social engineering and kidnapping to force Pix transfers
- **VPN for banking:** Protects your banking session on public networks
- **2FA on all financial apps:** Essential alongside VPN protection
- **Pix limits:** Many Brazilians set nighttime Pix limits to reduce kidnapping risk

## São Paulo Tech Scene

São Paulo is Latin America's largest tech hub:
- **Faria Lima corridor:** Brazil's Silicon Valley with hundreds of tech companies
- **Co-working:** WeWork, Spaces, IWG, and dozens of local spaces
- **Fiber broadband:** Vivo Fibra offers gigabit speeds in SP for R$150/month (~$30)
- **Digital nomad community:** Florianópolis remains the nomad favorite, but SP has the best infrastructure
