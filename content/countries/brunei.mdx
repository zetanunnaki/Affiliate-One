The oil-rich sultanate applies content filtering aligned with Islamic law and state ideology, blocking pornographic material, content critical of the monarchy, and sites deemed contrary to religious values. ## VPN Legality

VPN use is legal in Brunei for personal and business purposes. The government does not ban VPN software or prosecute users for encrypting their connections. However, accessing content that violates Sharia law or the Sedition Act remains illegal regardless of the method used. Expatriates and businesses use VPNs routinely.
