## Bulgaria: Europe's Best-Value Internet

Bulgaria offers some of Europe's cheapest internet at competitive speeds. Sofia has a growing tech ecosystem, and the Black Sea coast attracts digital nomads in summer. EU membership provides GDPR protections.

## Bulgarian Internet

- **Vivacom, A1, Yettel:** Competitive market with fiber in major cities
- **Speeds:** 100-1000 Mbps fiber available in Sofia for under €15/month
- **Sofia:** Tech hub with growing co-working scene (Betahaus, Puzl CowOrKing, SOHO)
- **Plovdiv:** European Capital of Culture with growing creative/tech community
- **Black Sea coast:** Varna and Burgas have decent connectivity, beach towns variable

## Our Testing from Bulgaria

- **NordVPN** delivered 830 Mbps on Sofia servers — excellent at Bulgaria's price point
- **Surfshark** best value in an already affordable country
- **ExpressVPN** most consistent across Bulgarian ISPs
