## Burkina Faso: VPN Guide

Burkina Faso has suspended social media during political transitions. Limited internet mainly in Ouagadougou. Security situation affects connectivity. VPN essential during political tensions.

## Why Use a VPN in Burkina Faso

- **Public Wi-Fi protection** — Encrypt traffic on shared networks
- **ISP privacy** — Prevent monitoring of your browsing activity
- **Content access** — Access home-country services while in Burkina Faso
- **Communication security** — Protect messaging and VoIP calls
