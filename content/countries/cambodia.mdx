## Cambodia: Southeast Asia's Affordable Frontier

Cambodia is one of the cheapest destinations for digital nomads in Southeast Asia. Phnom Penh and Siem Reap have growing expat communities with improving internet infrastructure. The USD-based economy simplifies finances for Western remote workers.

## Internet Reality

- **Smart, Metfone, Cellcard:** Major carriers with improving 4G coverage
- **Phnom Penh:** Best connectivity. Fiber available in central areas (30-100 Mbps typical)
- **Siem Reap:** Tourist-grade Wi-Fi near Angkor Wat area. Cafes and hotels generally connected
- **Cost:** Among the cheapest internet in SE Asia. $15-30/month for home broadband
- **USD economy:** Most transactions in USD, simplifying VPN subscription payments

## The Digital Nomad Scene

Cambodia offers ultra-affordable living:
- $500-800/month for comfortable living in Phnom Penh
- Growing co-working spaces in both Phnom Penh and Siem Reap
- Café culture with free Wi-Fi (always use VPN on shared networks)
- Easy visa situation with long-stay options available
