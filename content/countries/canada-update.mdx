## Canada 2026 Update: Privacy Reform Progress

Canada's privacy framework is evolving with Bill C-27 (Digital Charter Implementation Act) making progress. Quebec's Law 25 has already implemented GDPR-like protections at the provincial level. Federal reform aims to modernize PIPEDA for the digital age.

## Canadian Internet Pricing

Canada's internet remains among the most expensive in developed nations:
- **Bell, Rogers, Telus:** The big three maintain high prices due to limited competition
- **Smaller ISPs:** TekSavvy, Start.ca offer better value through wholesale access
- **5G expansion:** Growing in urban areas. Rural Canada still depends on Starlink
- **Tim Hortons Wi-Fi:** Canada's de facto public Wi-Fi network — always use VPN

## Five Eyes and Canadian Privacy

As a Five Eyes founding member, Canada's intelligence agencies participate in signals intelligence sharing. The CSE (Communications Security Establishment) has broad surveillance capabilities. A VPN from a non-Five Eyes jurisdiction adds meaningful privacy.
