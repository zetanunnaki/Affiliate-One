## Chile: Latin America's Digital Leader

Chile has the best internet infrastructure in Latin America. Strong net neutrality laws (Chile was the first country globally to enact net neutrality in 2010), high broadband penetration, and competitive pricing make it ideal for remote work.

## Why Chile Stands Out

- **Net neutrality pioneer:** Chile's 2010 law prevents ISP throttling and blocking
- **Best speeds in LatAm:** Average broadband exceeds 200 Mbps in Santiago
- **Competitive pricing:** Fiber from Movistar, Entel, and Claro at reasonable rates
- **No censorship:** Chile has one of the freest internet environments in the Americas

## Santiago for Remote Work

Chile's capital is a major tech hub:
- **Co-working:** WeWork, Nex (local chain), and many independent spaces
- **Café culture:** Barrio Italia, Lastarria, and Providencia neighborhoods have excellent café Wi-Fi
- **Valparaíso:** Colorful port city with growing creative and tech community
- **Internet:** 500 Mbps+ fiber widely available in Santiago for ~$25/month
