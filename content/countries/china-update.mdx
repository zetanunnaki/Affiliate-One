## China 2026 Update: The Firewall Gets Smarter

The Great Firewall continues to evolve. In 2026, the GFW uses machine learning-based deep packet inspection that adapts faster to new VPN obfuscation techniques. The result: VPN reliability fluctuates more than ever, with some weeks better than others.

## Current Blocking Status (April 2026)

**Still blocked:**
- Google (all services), Facebook, Instagram, WhatsApp, Twitter/X, YouTube, Wikipedia
- Slack, Notion, Discord, Telegram
- Most Western news outlets
- Many VPN provider websites

**Working (without VPN):**
- WeChat, Weibo, Douyin (Chinese TikTok), Taobao, Alipay
- Apple services (iCloud works with Chinese data center)
- Microsoft services (Azure China, some Office 365)
- LinkedIn (relaunched as limited version)

## Updated Daily Tips

- **Morning routine:** Connect VPN immediately after waking up. Test Gmail, Slack before starting work
- **Protocol switching:** If your primary fails, cycle through: obfuscated > OpenVPN TCP 443 > Shadowsocks > Tor bridges
- **Hong Kong servers:** Generally best speed/reliability balance from mainland China
- **Peak hours:** VPN reliability drops during evening hours (7-11 PM) when most people are online
- **Sensitive dates:** Expect lower reliability around June 4, National Congress, and other politically sensitive periods
