## Costa Rica: Pura Vida Remote Work

Costa Rica is Central America's most stable and connected country. Its "pura vida" lifestyle, biodiversity, and growing digital nomad visa program attract remote workers seeking work-life balance in a tropical setting.

## Digital Infrastructure

- **Kolbi (ICE), Claro, Movistar:** Competitive mobile market with good 4G coverage
- **San José area:** Best broadband. Fiber available in metro area with speeds up to 300 Mbps
- **Beach towns:** Tamarindo, Santa Teresa, and Puerto Viejo have decent but variable Wi-Fi. Co-working spaces are the most reliable option
- **Eco-lodges:** Connectivity varies dramatically. Cloud forest and remote eco-lodges may have limited or no internet

## The Digital Nomad Scene

Costa Rica's digital nomad visa (Rentista) allows stays up to 2 years:
- **San José:** Best infrastructure, most co-working options
- **Santa Teresa:** Beach town popular with surfing nomads. Growing co-working scene
- **Tamarindo:** More developed beach town with better infrastructure
- **USD accepted widely** — Costa Rica's economy is semi-dollarized, simplifying VPN purchases
