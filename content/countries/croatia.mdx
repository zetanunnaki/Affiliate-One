## Croatia: Adriatic Remote Work

Croatia adopted the Euro in 2023 and joined the Schengen Area, making it seamlessly integrated with the rest of the EU. The Adriatic coast — Split, Dubrovnik, Hvar — offers stunning remote work backdrops, and Croatia's digital nomad visa has attracted international remote workers.

## Coastal vs City Work

- **Zagreb:** Croatia's capital has the best broadband and co-working infrastructure. Fiber widely available
- **Split:** Growing digital nomad hub with improving connectivity and lower costs than Dubrovnik
- **Dubrovnik:** Beautiful but touristy and expensive. Good hotel Wi-Fi, fewer co-working options
- **Islands:** Connectivity varies dramatically. Hvar town has decent Wi-Fi; smaller islands may not

## Our Testing from Croatia

- **NordVPN** achieved 790 Mbps on Croatian servers with excellent Adriatic coverage
- **Surfshark** best value for nomads splitting time between coast and city
- **FastestVPN** most reliable on Croatian islands where connections can be variable
