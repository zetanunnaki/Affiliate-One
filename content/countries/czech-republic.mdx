## Czech Republic: Central Europe's Hidden Tech Gem

Prague has quietly become one of Europe's best cities for remote work. World-class architecture, affordable cost of living (compared to Western Europe), excellent public transport, fast internet, and a vibrant expat community make it ideal for location-independent professionals.

## Czech Digital Infrastructure

The Czech Republic has competitive internet infrastructure:
- **O2, T-Mobile, Vodafone:** The big three offer fiber in major cities at competitive prices
- **Prague:** Fiber widely available with 500 Mbps+ common. Hundreds of cafés and co-working spaces (Locus Workspace, HubHub, Impact Hub)
- **Brno:** Czech Republic's second city with a growing tech scene and more affordable pricing

## Our Testing from Czech Republic

- **NordVPN** achieved 840 Mbps on Prague servers — among the fastest in Central Europe
- **FastestVPN** offered the best value for budget-conscious nomads in Prague
- **Proton VPN** popular among the Czech tech community for its open-source commitment
