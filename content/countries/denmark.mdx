## Denmark: Scandinavian Digital Excellence

Denmark consistently ranks among the most digitally advanced countries in the world. Nearly cashless, highly connected, and with universal high-speed broadband, Denmark offers an ideal environment for remote work. Copenhagen's tech scene rivals Stockholm and Helsinki.

## Danish Internet and Privacy

Denmark balances strong digital infrastructure with Scandinavian privacy values. However, as an EU member, Danish ISPs are subject to data retention requirements. The Danish intelligence service (PET/FE) participates in European intelligence sharing.

Danish users benefit from GDPR protections enforced by the Danish Data Protection Agency (Datatilsynet), but a VPN adds an additional privacy layer that prevents ISP-level monitoring.

## Why Use a VPN in Denmark

- **Data retention:** Danish ISPs retain connection data under EU requirements
- **MobilePay everywhere:** Denmark's nearly cashless economy means digital privacy is especially important
- **Excellent public Wi-Fi:** Copenhagen, Aarhus, and Odense have extensive free Wi-Fi — shared and unencrypted
- **DSB trains:** Danish State Railways offers Wi-Fi, but it's shared among all passengers
