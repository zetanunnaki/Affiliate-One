## Internet Freedom & Privacy Landscape

Denmark scores **97/100** on Freedom House's Freedom on the Net index, making it one of the highest-ranked nations in the world for internet freedom and placing it firmly in the "Free" category. The country is a global leader in digital governance, with nearly universal high-speed broadband coverage and average fixed-line speeds exceeding 300 Mbps. Major ISPs including TDC (YouSee), Telenor, Telia, and Stofa compete in a market that delivers fiber and cable connections up to 1 Gbps for 200-400 DKK per month.

However, Denmark's intelligence agencies have been at the center of significant surveillance controversies. In 2021, revelations confirmed that the Danish military intelligence service FE (Forsvarets Efterretningstjeneste) helped the US National Security Agency spy on European leaders including German Chancellor Angela Merkel. The domestic security service PET (Politiets Efterretningstjeneste) operates with broad surveillance capabilities. Danish ISPs are required to retain session logging data under the Logningsbekendtgoerelsen, which mandates storage of connection metadata for up to one year. The Danish Data Protection Agency (Datatilsynet) enforces GDPR rigorously, but these protections do not prevent intelligence-level surveillance or ISP data collection.

## VPN Legality

VPN use is entirely legal in Denmark with no restrictions, registration requirements, or gray areas. Danish law protects freedom of communication and expression, and VPNs are widely used by businesses, universities, and privacy-conscious individuals. No ISP blocks or throttles VPN traffic, and the government has never proposed legislation restricting VPN access. Denmark's strong rule of law and democratic institutions mean there is zero risk of enforcement action for VPN usage regardless of purpose.

## Why You Need a VPN in Denmark

- **Session logging mandate** — Danish ISPs retain connection metadata under the Logningsbekendtgoerelsen for up to one year, creating a detailed record of your online activity including IP addresses, timestamps, and destination servers accessible to law enforcement with minimal judicial oversight
- **Nine Eyes intelligence sharing** — Denmark participates in the Nine Eyes alliance, meaning surveillance data collected domestically may be shared with the US, UK, Canada, Australia, New Zealand, and additional partner nations without individual notification or consent
- **FE surveillance capabilities** — Danish military intelligence has demonstrated willingness to conduct mass surveillance even of allied nations, as confirmed by the 2021 revelations about NSA cooperation
- **Nearly cashless economy** — Denmark's MobilePay-dominated transaction ecosystem means virtually every purchase creates a digital footprint, and a VPN protects the browsing activity surrounding your financial decisions from correlation
- **Ubiquitous public Wi-Fi** — Copenhagen, Aarhus, and Odense have extensive free Wi-Fi in libraries, public spaces, cafes, and DSB train services that are shared among all users without encryption

## Best VPN Protocols for Denmark

Denmark's open internet environment and absence of VPN blocking or throttling means protocol selection is driven purely by performance optimization. WireGuard-based protocols deliver the best results on Danish high-speed connections, with NordLynx achieving near-wire speeds on fiber infrastructure. OpenVPN (UDP) provides maximum compatibility for users connecting through corporate firewalls or legacy network equipment. IKEv2/IPsec is the optimal choice for mobile users on Telia, Telenor, or 3 (Three) cellular networks, offering seamless handoff between Wi-Fi and mobile data without connection drops. No obfuscation or stealth protocols are necessary in Denmark, as no entity monitors for or restricts VPN protocol signatures.
