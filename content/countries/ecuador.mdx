## VPN Legality

VPN usage is legal in Ecuador for personal and commercial purposes. There are no laws restricting encryption tools, and many international businesses and NGOs operating in Ecuador use VPNs as standard security practice for their communications.

## Best VPN Protocols for Ecuador

WireGuard is essential for Ecuador where every millisecond of protocol overhead matters given the baseline latency to nearest servers. The protocol's faster handshake also improves reconnection on Ecuador's mobile networks (Claro, Movistar) where coverage transitions between towers are frequent in mountainous terrain. For users on CNT connections that sometimes throttle VPN traffic, SSTP or OpenVPN TCP over port 443 provides stealth connectivity that avoids detection.
