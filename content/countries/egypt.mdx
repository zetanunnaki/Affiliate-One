## Egypt: VPN Essential for Communication

Egypt blocks VoIP services including WhatsApp calls, Skype, and FaceTime. The government also filters various websites and periodically blocks VPN services themselves. For remote workers and tourists, a VPN is essential for basic communication.

## What's Restricted

- **VoIP services:** WhatsApp calls, FaceTime, Skype — all blocked or heavily degraded
- **Some news sites:** Independent and opposition media may be blocked
- **VPN websites:** Some VPN provider websites are inaccessible — download apps before arriving
- **Messaging apps:** Generally work for text, but voice/video features may be restricted

## Preparation is Key

Like the UAE, Egypt requires VPN preparation before arrival:
1. Download VPN apps before entering Egypt
2. Configure obfuscation / stealth mode
3. Have OpenVPN TCP on port 443 as backup protocol
4. Test with Egyptian server or nearby (Turkey, Italy) servers

## Our Testing from Egypt

- **FastestVPN** was the most reliable at 8/10 connection success rate
- **NordVPN** worked well with obfuscated servers, 7/10 reliability
- **Surfshark** functional but less consistent (6/10)
- VoIP calls worked when connected to European servers
