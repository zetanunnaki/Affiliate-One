## Estonia: The World's Most Digital Country

Estonia is a digital pioneer. E-Residency, digital ID, i-Voting, e-Tax — nearly every government service is available online. The country experienced one of the world's first cyberattacks on national infrastructure (2007), which drove massive investment in cybersecurity.

For remote workers, Estonia is uniquely attractive: the e-Residency program lets you establish an EU business remotely, and Tallinn's tech scene punches far above its weight.

## Why Estonia Matters for VPN Users

Estonia's digital-first culture means more of your life is online than almost anywhere else. This makes digital security not optional but essential:

- **Digital ID:** Nearly every Estonian transaction uses digital identity. Protecting this identity is critical
- **e-Residency:** The 100,000+ e-Residents worldwide manage businesses through Estonian digital infrastructure — all requiring secure connections
- **Post-2007 cybersecurity focus:** Estonia invested heavily in cybersecurity after the 2007 attacks, making it one of the most cyber-aware populations globally
- **NATO CCDCOE:** Tallinn hosts NATO's Cooperative Cyber Defence Centre of Excellence
