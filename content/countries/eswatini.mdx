## Eswatini: VPN Guide

Eswatini (formerly Swaziland) has limited internet mainly in Mbabane. Government has restricted social media during political tensions. Small country with concentrated infrastructure.

- **ISP privacy** — Prevent monitoring of your browsing activity
- **Content access** — Access home-country services while in Eswatini
- **Communication security** — Protect messaging and VoIP calls
