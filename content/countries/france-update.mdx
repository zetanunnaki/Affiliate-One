## France 2026 Update: ARCOM Gets Teeth

HADOPI has been replaced by ARCOM (Autorité de régulation de la communication audiovisuelle et numérique), which has broader powers including anti-piracy enforcement. The graduated response system continues to send warnings to file sharers, making VPN use for P2P activity standard practice in France.
