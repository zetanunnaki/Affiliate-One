## Gambia: VPN Guide

The Gambia imposed a complete internet shutdown during the 2016 elections, driving VPN awareness. English-speaking. Beach resort areas have tourist-grade Wi-Fi.

## Why Use a VPN in Gambia

- **Public Wi-Fi protection** — Encrypt traffic on shared networks
- **ISP privacy** — Prevent monitoring of your browsing activity
- **Content access** — Access home-country services while in Gambia
- **Communication security** — Protect messaging and VoIP calls
