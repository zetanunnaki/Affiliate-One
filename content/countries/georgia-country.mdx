## Georgia: The Caucasus' Hidden Gem

Georgia has quietly become one of the world's best-value destinations for digital nomads. Visa-free for most nationalities (365-day stay for many passport holders), incredibly affordable, with fast internet and a unique wine-and-food culture.

## Why Digital Nomads Love Georgia

- **No visa needed:** Citizens of 95+ countries can stay up to 1 year without a visa
- **Ultra-affordable:** $500-800/month for comfortable living in Tbilisi including housing
- **Fast, cheap internet:** 100 Mbps fiber for ~$10/month. Some of the cheapest broadband globally
- **Free and open internet:** No censorship, no restrictions, no VPN blocking
- **Amazing food and wine:** Georgia claims to be the birthplace of wine (8,000 years)

## Tbilisi for Remote Work

Georgia's capital has excellent nomad infrastructure:
- **Co-working:** Terminal, Impact Hub, Loft Tbilisi, plus many café-offices
- **Café culture:** Strong. Georgian cafés welcome laptop workers and many have fast Wi-Fi
- **Magti, Beeline, Geocell:** Competitive providers with affordable fiber and mobile data
- **Batumi:** Black Sea coast alternative with growing tech scene and seaside co-working
