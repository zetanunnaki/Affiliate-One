## Georgia 2026 Update: Nomad Capital of the Caucasus

Georgia continues to cement its position as one of the world's best-value digital nomad destinations. Visa-free for 95+ nationalities for up to a year, with some of the world's cheapest broadband and an incredible food/wine culture.

## Why Nomads Keep Coming

- **$500-800/month** for comfortable Tbilisi living including housing
- **$10/month** for 100 Mbps fiber broadband — among the world's cheapest
- **365-day visa-free** stays for most passport holders
- **No income tax on foreign income** for the first year (under certain conditions)
- **8,000 years of wine** culture plus incredible cuisine (khinkali, khachapuri)

## Co-Working Explosion

Tbilisi's co-working scene has matured:
- **Terminal, Impact Hub, Loft:** Established spaces with reliable infrastructure
- **Dozens of café-offices** in Vake, Vera, and Saburtalo neighborhoods
- **Batumi:** Black Sea coast alternative with lower costs and growing nomad scene
- **Kutaisi:** Third city gaining attention from budget nomads

## 2026 Testing Update

- **NordVPN** delivers 700 Mbps from Tbilisi — excellent for the price point
- **FastestVPN** matches Georgia's affordability — unlimited devices at $2.29/mo
- **FastestVPN** best if combining Georgia with trips to restrictive neighbors (Turkey, Azerbaijan)
