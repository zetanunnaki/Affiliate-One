## Ghana: West Africa's Digital Star

Ghana stands out in West Africa for its stable democracy, English-speaking population, and growing tech sector. Accra has a vibrant startup ecosystem often called the "Silicon Savannah of West Africa" (alongside Nairobi), with improving internet infrastructure.

## Ghanaian Internet Landscape

- **MTN, Vodafone, AirtelTigo:** Competitive mobile market with expanding 4G coverage
- **Accra:** Best connectivity. Fiber available in key areas (Osu, East Legon, Airport City)
- **Mobile money:** MTN MoMo dominates. Critical for daily transactions
- **Co-working:** Impact Hub Accra, Regus, and local spaces serving the growing tech community
- **Cape Coast and Kumasi:** Decent connectivity in urban centers, limited in rural areas
