## Greece: Mediterranean Remote Work Paradise

Greece has become an increasingly popular digital nomad destination, offering a digital nomad visa, affordable living (especially outside Athens), stunning islands, and improving internet infrastructure. Athens, Thessaloniki, and Crete are the primary remote work hubs.

## Internet Reality

Greek internet has improved significantly but still lags behind Northern Europe:
- **COSMOTE (OTE), Vodafone, Nova:** Major providers. Fiber expanding in Athens and major cities with speeds up to 1 Gbps
- **Island connectivity:** Varies dramatically. Santorini and Mykonos tourist areas have decent Wi-Fi. Smaller islands may have limited bandwidth, especially in peak summer season
- **Café culture:** Strong in Athens and island towns. Most tavernas and cafés offer free Wi-Fi

## Our Testing from Greece

- **NordVPN** achieved 750 Mbps on Athens servers with good coverage across the Mediterranean
- **FastestVPN** was the most reliable on Greek islands where connection quality can be variable
- **Surfshark** best value for long-stay nomads enjoying the Greek digital nomad visa
