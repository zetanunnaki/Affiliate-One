## Guinea: VPN Guide

Guinea's internet is limited with government shutdowns during political events. Coverage mainly in Conakry. VPN use is common during social media blocks.

## Why Use a VPN in Guinea

- **Public Wi-Fi protection** — Encrypt traffic on shared networks
- **ISP privacy** — Prevent monitoring of your browsing activity
- **Content access** — Access home-country services while in Guinea
- **Communication security** — Protect messaging and VoIP calls
