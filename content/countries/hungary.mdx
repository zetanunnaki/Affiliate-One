## Hungary: Budapest's Digital Nomad Paradise

Budapest has established itself as one of Central Europe's top digital nomad destinations. The combination of affordable living, world-class thermal baths, ruin bars, and fast internet attracts thousands of remote workers annually. The city offers excellent value compared to Western European capitals.

## Budapest for Remote Workers

- **Co-working spaces:** Dozens of options including Kaptar, Mosaik, and Hub Hub at €50-150/month
- **Café culture:** Budapest's café scene is legendary. Most offer free Wi-Fi and welcome laptop workers
- **Internet:** Magyar Telekom, Vodafone, and Yettel offer fiber up to 1 Gbps in Budapest for reasonable prices
- **Ruin bars:** Even Budapest's famous ruin bars increasingly offer Wi-Fi (though we don't recommend mixing work and palinka)
- **Thermal baths:** Some baths now offer Wi-Fi in relaxation areas. The ultimate "work from anywhere" flex

## Our Testing from Hungary

- **NordVPN** delivered 830 Mbps on Budapest servers with excellent European coverage
- **Surfshark** best value for nomads doing extended stays in Hungary
- **FastestVPN** most consistent across Hungarian ISPs and mobile networks
