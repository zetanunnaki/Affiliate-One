## Iceland: The Privacy-Friendly Power Hub

Iceland combines strong privacy laws, abundant geothermal energy, and cool climate — making it an ideal location for data centers. Several privacy-focused companies have established operations here, attracted by Iceland's constitutional privacy protections and favorable data center conditions.

## Icelandic Internet

- **Síminn, Vodafone, Nova:** Competitive market with fiber expanding across the island
- **Nearly 100% penetration:** Iceland has one of the highest internet adoption rates globally
- **Data centers:** Iceland hosts data centers for privacy-focused services, powered by 100% renewable energy
- **Remote areas:** The highland interior and some fjord communities have limited connectivity. Ring Road generally well-covered

## Why Iceland Matters for Privacy

Iceland's privacy credentials:
- Constitutional right to privacy
- Strong data protection laws (aligned with GDPR through EEA)
- No mandatory data retention laws for ISPs
- Proton VPN routes Secure Core traffic through Iceland
- Not in NATO intelligence-sharing frameworks beyond standard ISAF cooperation
