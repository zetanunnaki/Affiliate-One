## Indonesia: Bali's Digital Nomad Paradise (With Restrictions)

Indonesia — particularly Bali — is one of the world's top digital nomad destinations. Ubud and Canggu have massive expat communities with excellent co-working spaces. However, Indonesia blocks various websites and social media has been throttled during political unrest.

## What's Blocked

Indonesia blocks a range of content:
- **Reddit** — Completely blocked
- **Adult content** — Extensively blocked
- **Gambling sites** — Blocked
- **Some streaming platforms** — Occasionally restricted
- **Social media** — Has been throttled during protests (2019 Papua unrest)

VPN use is legal and extremely common among Indonesia's tech-savvy population and massive expat community.

## The Bali Remote Work Scene

Bali is digital nomad central:
- **Canggu:** The epicenter. Dozens of co-working spaces (Dojo Bali, Outpost, Tropical Nomad). Excellent café Wi-Fi
- **Ubud:** More zen, fewer parties. Good co-working (Hubud was legendary, many successors)
- **Seminyak/Kuta:** Tourist-focused but workable
- **Internet:** Fiber from IndiHome (Telkom) available in popular areas. 50-100 Mbps typical. Can be inconsistent
