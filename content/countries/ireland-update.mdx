## Ireland 2026 Update: DPC Gets Tougher

Ireland's Data Protection Commission has significantly ramped up GDPR enforcement, issuing over €3 billion in fines against Big Tech companies since 2020. For Irish residents and the massive international community in Dublin, this means stronger theoretical privacy protections — though VPN use remains essential for practical security.

## The Remote Work Revolution

Ireland has fully embraced remote and hybrid work with government support:
- **Remote Work Strategy:** Ireland's national strategy encourages remote work across the country
- **Connected Hubs:** 400+ government-funded remote working hubs across rural Ireland
- **Dublin remains dominant** for tech jobs but Cork, Galway, and Limerick are growing
- **Broadband improvement:** The National Broadband Plan is bringing fiber to rural areas

## Irish Café and Pub Wi-Fi Culture

Ireland's famous pub culture now includes Wi-Fi:
- Nearly every pub, café, and restaurant offers free Wi-Fi
- Train services (Irish Rail) and buses (Bus Éireann) increasingly offer Wi-Fi
- Dublin's city center has extensive public Wi-Fi coverage
- All of these are shared networks — VPN essential
