## Israel: The Cybersecurity Superpower

Israel punches far above its weight in cybersecurity. Per capita, Israel has more cybersecurity companies and more cybersecurity expertise than any other country. This creates both a highly tech-savvy population and a government with significant surveillance capabilities.

## Why Use a VPN in Israel

- **Unit 8200 alumni:** Israel's signals intelligence unit produces many of the country's tech entrepreneurs. The government has sophisticated surveillance capabilities
- **ISP monitoring:** Israeli ISPs are required to maintain interception capabilities. A VPN prevents routine traffic monitoring
- **Wi-Fi security:** Israel has excellent free Wi-Fi coverage — but shared networks carry risks. Tel Aviv, Jerusalem, and Haifa have extensive public Wi-Fi
- **Regional content access:** Access Israeli streaming (Keshet, Kan) and banking while traveling abroad

## Israel's Tech Infrastructure

Israel has excellent internet infrastructure:
- **Bezeq, Partner (012), Cellcom:** Major broadband providers with fiber expanding
- **Hot:** Cable provider with fast broadband
- **5G coverage:** Rapidly expanding, particularly in the Tel Aviv metro area
- **Startup ecosystem:** Tel Aviv's "Silicon Wadi" is second only to Silicon Valley in tech density

## Our Testing from Israel

- **NordVPN** delivered 800 Mbps on Israeli servers with excellent latency to European destinations
- **ExpressVPN** had the most consistent performance across Israel including Haifa and Beersheba
- **Proton VPN** popular among Israel's privacy-conscious tech community
