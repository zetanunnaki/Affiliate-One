## Italy: Ancient History, Modern Connectivity

Italy is investing heavily in broadband expansion, with fiber reaching most major cities and 5G rolling out nationwide. The country's digital nomad visa (introduced 2024) and affordable living outside Milan and Rome make it increasingly attractive for remote workers.

## Italian Privacy and Internet Landscape

Italy's data protection authority (Garante per la protezione dei dati personali) is one of Europe's most active GDPR enforcers. Italy was the first country to temporarily ban ChatGPT over privacy concerns (2023), demonstrating aggressive privacy enforcement.

Italian ISPs are required to comply with court-ordered content blocking, primarily targeting piracy and gambling sites. A VPN bypasses these blocks for legitimate content access.

## Remote Work in Italy

- **Milan:** Italy's business and tech capital with excellent co-working infrastructure and fiber broadband
- **Rome:** Growing startup scene with beautiful working environments. Many cafés in historic center offer Wi-Fi
- **Florence & Bologna:** University cities with strong digital infrastructure and affordable living
- **Southern Italy & Islands:** More affordable but internet quality can be variable in smaller towns

## Our Testing from Italy

- **NordVPN** achieved 810 Mbps on Milan servers (near Italy's major internet exchanges)
- **FastestVPN** had the most reliable performance including from smaller Italian cities and Sardinia/Sicily
- **Proton VPN** popular with privacy-conscious Italian users given Italy's strong privacy culture
