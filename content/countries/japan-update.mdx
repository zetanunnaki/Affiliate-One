## Japan 2026 Update: Wi-Fi Nation

Japan's public Wi-Fi coverage has expanded dramatically for tourists:
- **Japan Connected Wi-Fi:** 300,000+ hotspots (up from 200,000)
- **Shinkansen Wi-Fi:** Now available on most bullet train routes
- **Convenience stores:** 7-Eleven, FamilyMart, Lawson all offer free Wi-Fi
- **Tourist SIM/eSIM:** Affordable data options from multiple providers
