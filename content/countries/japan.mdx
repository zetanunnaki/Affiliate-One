## Japan's World-Class Internet

Japan boasts some of the fastest and most reliable internet infrastructure in the world. With average download speeds exceeding 200 Mbps and widespread fiber-to-the-home deployment, Japan is a paradise for remote workers — as long as you secure your connection.

## Why Use a VPN in Japan

Japan has minimal internet censorship and strong consumer protections, but there are several reasons to use a VPN:

- **Public Wi-Fi is everywhere but insecure:** Convenience stores (7-Eleven, FamilyMart, Lawson), train stations, and cafés all offer free Wi-Fi. Most networks are open and unencrypted
- **Accessing home services while visiting:** If you're a tourist or business traveler, you'll need a VPN to access streaming services from your home country
- **Privacy from ISPs:** Japanese ISPs comply with government requests for subscriber data. A VPN prevents your ISP from logging your browsing activity
- **Remote work security:** Japan's growing remote work culture means more people working from cafés and co-working spaces on shared networks

## Public Wi-Fi in Japan

Japan has invested heavily in public Wi-Fi, particularly for tourists:

- **Japan Connected-free Wi-Fi:** App that aggregates free Wi-Fi access points across Japan. Over 200,000 hotspots
- **Station Wi-Fi:** JR East, Tokyo Metro, and other train operators offer free Wi-Fi at stations
- **Convenience store Wi-Fi:** 7-Eleven, FamilyMart, and Lawson all provide free Wi-Fi (time-limited)
- **Starbucks Japan:** Free Wi-Fi, popular with remote workers
- **Pocket Wi-Fi rentals:** Portable hotspots are popular with tourists. Renting one gives you a more secure personal network than public Wi-Fi

While convenient, all these networks are shared. Always use a VPN.

## Japan-Specific Considerations

- **NHK and Japanese streaming:** Access TVer, Abema, and NHK World from abroad with a VPN connected to a Japanese server
- **Gaming:** Japan's gaming culture means low latency matters. WireGuard protocol offers the best gaming performance
- **Payment:** Credit cards are increasingly accepted but cash (yen) remains important. VPN subscriptions should be purchased online before arrival
- **Language barrier:** Most VPN apps have Japanese language support. NordVPN and FastestVPN have dedicated Japanese customer support

## Our Testing from Japan

Tested from Tokyo, Osaka, and Fukuoka:

- **NordVPN** achieved 850 Mbps on Tokyo servers, the fastest we tested in the APAC region
- **FastestVPN** was the most reliable for accessing blocked content in neighboring countries (useful for travel to China or other restricted areas)
- **Surfshark** offered the best value for budget-conscious users
- Latency to Tokyo servers was under 5ms from local connections — essentially unnoticeable
