## Latvia: The Quiet Baltic Tech Hub

Latvia, particularly Riga, has a strong IT tradition and growing startup ecosystem. Good broadband infrastructure, EU membership, and affordable living make it attractive for tech professionals and remote workers.

## Latvian Digital Infrastructure

- **Tet, Bite, LMT:** Competitive broadband market with fiber expanding rapidly
- **Riga:** Excellent connectivity with fiber available in most neighborhoods. Growing co-working scene
- **Speeds:** 100-500 Mbps fiber widely available at competitive prices
- **Free Wi-Fi:** Riga has extensive public Wi-Fi coverage in the Old Town and central areas
