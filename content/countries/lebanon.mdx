## Lebanon: Connectivity Under Crisis

Lebanon's economic crisis has severely impacted telecommunications infrastructure. Power outages lasting 20+ hours daily affect internet access, and the banking crisis has complicated digital payments. Despite these challenges, the Lebanese tech community has shown remarkable resilience.

## Internet Challenges

- **Power outages:** Chronic electricity shortages require generators for internet access. Many cafés and co-working spaces have their own generators
- **Infrastructure decay:** OGERO (state telecom) struggles to maintain infrastructure. Connection quality has declined significantly
- **ISPs:** Ogero, Alfa, Touch (mobile). Quality and availability vary by area
- **Costs:** Dollar-denominated internet costs have become proportionally expensive due to currency collapse

## Why VPN in Lebanon

- **VoIP access:** Some VoIP services have been restricted. VPN ensures access to WhatsApp calls and other communication tools
- **Privacy:** Given political instability, privacy is important for many Lebanese users
- **Content access:** Access Lebanese content abroad, or international content locally
- **Reliable communication:** VPN can route around network issues by connecting to more stable international servers

## Our Testing for Lebanon

- **ExpressVPN** most reliable for maintaining connections through Lebanon's unstable infrastructure
- **Surfshark** best value given economic conditions — unlimited devices at the lowest price
- **NordVPN** good speeds when connection is available, with servers in nearby Cyprus and Turkey
