## Lesotho: VPN Guide

Lesotho has limited internet mainly in Maseru. This mountainous kingdom has challenging terrain for infrastructure. South African rand accepted alongside local currency.

## Why Use a VPN in Lesotho

- **Public Wi-Fi protection** — Encrypt traffic on shared networks
- **ISP privacy** — Prevent monitoring of your browsing activity
- **Content access** — Access home-country services while in Lesotho
- **Communication security** — Protect messaging and VoIP calls
