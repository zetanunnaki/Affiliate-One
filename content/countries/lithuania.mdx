## Lithuania: The Baltic Fintech Capital

Lithuania has positioned itself as Europe's fintech hub, with more fintech licenses than any other EU country. Vilnius has a growing tech ecosystem with excellent digital infrastructure and affordable living compared to Western Europe.

## Lithuanian Digital Landscape

- **Telia, Bite, Tele2:** Competitive ISP market with fiber expanding rapidly
- **Vilnius:** Excellent connectivity with fiber available in most areas. Growing co-working scene
- **Kaunas:** Lithuania's second city with a strong tech university presence
- **EU membership:** GDPR protections, Schengen access, and eurozone membership
