## Macau: China's Exception

Macau operates under "One Country, Two Systems" with a separate internet regime from mainland China. Unlike the mainland, Google, Facebook, WhatsApp, and most international services work WITHOUT a VPN. The Great Firewall does not extend to Macau.

## Key Difference from Mainland China

- **Google, Facebook, YouTube:** All work normally in Macau
- **WhatsApp, Instagram, Twitter:** All accessible without VPN
- **Western news sites:** Not blocked
- **VPN websites:** Accessible and downloadable
- **Casino Wi-Fi:** World-class — casinos invest heavily in guest connectivity

This makes Macau a useful staging point for travelers heading to mainland China: download and configure your VPN while in Macau before crossing to the mainland.

## Why Use a VPN in Macau

Despite the open internet, a VPN is still useful:
- **Casino Wi-Fi security:** Shared networks with thousands of users
- **Privacy from ISPs:** CTM can still monitor your browsing
- **Content access:** Access home-country streaming while visiting
- **Mainland China preparation:** Configure and test your VPN in Macau before entering the mainland
