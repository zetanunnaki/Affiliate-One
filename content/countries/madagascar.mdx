## Madagascar: VPN Guide

Madagascar has limited internet primarily in Antananarivo. National parks and wildlife areas have no connectivity. Growing mobile adoption. Download everything offline before leaving the capital.

## Why Use a VPN in Madagascar

- **Public Wi-Fi protection** — Encrypt traffic on shared networks
- **ISP privacy** — Prevent monitoring of your browsing activity
- **Content access** — Access home-country services while in Madagascar
- **Communication security** — Protect messaging and VoIP calls
