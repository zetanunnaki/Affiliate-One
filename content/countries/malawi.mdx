## Malawi: VPN Guide

Malawi has limited internet primarily in Lilongwe and Blantyre. Lake Malawi lodges increasingly offer satellite Wi-Fi. Government has throttled social media during elections.

## Why Use a VPN in Malawi

- **Public Wi-Fi protection** — Encrypt traffic on shared networks
- **ISP privacy** — Prevent monitoring of your browsing activity
- **Content access** — Access home-country services while in Malawi
- **Communication security** — Protect messaging and VoIP calls
