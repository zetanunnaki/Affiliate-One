## Mali: VPN Guide

Mali has limited internet with government-imposed shutdowns during political events. Coverage mainly in Bamako. Security situation affects infrastructure. VPN essential during restriction periods.

- **ISP privacy** — Prevent monitoring of your browsing activity
- **Content access** — Access home-country services while in Mali
- **Communication security** — Protect messaging and VoIP calls
