## Internet Freedom & Privacy Landscape

Malta is not individually assessed in Freedom House's Freedom on the Net report, but the country ranks in the middle tier of the Reporters Without Borders Press Freedom Index (around 73rd globally). As an EU member state, Malta benefits from GDPR protections, though concerns persist about government surveillance following revelations about Pegasus spyware deployment and the aftermath of journalist Daphne Caruana Galizia's assassination in 2017. The Malta Communications Authority (MCA) regulates the sector but political interference remains a concern.

Local ISPs: GO plc (formerly Maltacom), Melita Limited, and Epic Communications (formerly Vodafone Malta) serve the island. GO dominates fixed broadband through its fiber and DSL network, while Melita competes with cable and fiber infrastructure. Malta's small geographic size (316 km2) means comprehensive coverage across both Malta and Gozo islands, with average broadband speeds around 120 Mbps. ISPs comply with EU data retention requirements and Maltese law enforcement data requests.

## VPN Legality

VPNs are fully legal in Malta without any restrictions. The country's position as the world's leading iGaming regulatory hub means VPN technology is well-understood by regulators and lawmakers. Malta's Gaming Authority (MGA) regulates hundreds of online gambling companies that operate with sophisticated network infrastructure, creating a tech-savvy regulatory environment that does not view VPNs as threatening.

## Best VPN Protocols for Malta

WireGuard is the recommended protocol for Malta, providing excellent speeds through Italian servers with minimal overhead. The protocol works particularly well on both GO fiber and Melita cable connections, consistently delivering speeds above 130 Mbps. OpenVPN (UDP) serves as a reliable alternative for iGaming professionals whose corporate security policies require it for compliance with MGA regulatory standards. IKEv2 excels on mobile connections when moving between outdoor Wi-Fi hotspots and Epic mobile data.
