## Mexico 2026 Update: CDMX Dominates

Mexico City has cemented its position as Latin America's #1 digital nomad destination:
- **Same time zones as US:** Central Time makes it perfect for US-based remote work
- **$1,000-1,500/month:** Comfortable living in Roma, Condesa, or Polanco
- **300+ co-working spaces:** From WeWork to local gems like Centraal and Homework
- **Fiber broadband:** Izzi, Totalplay, and Telmex offer 200-500 Mbps in CDMX
- **OXXO Wi-Fi:** 20,000+ convenience stores with free Wi-Fi (always use VPN)

## Beyond CDMX

Other Mexican cities gaining nomad traction:
- **Playa del Carmen:** Caribbean coast, growing tech community, fiber available
- **Oaxaca:** Cultural capital with lower costs, improving connectivity
- **Mérida:** Colonial charm, affordable, improving broadband
- **Guadalajara:** Mexico's tech city with Silicon Valley companies (Oracle, IBM, Intel)
- **Puerto Vallarta:** Beach + work combination with decent infrastructure

## Security Considerations

Mexico has elevated online fraud rates:
- **Phishing targeting Mexican banks:** Banorte, BBVA Mexico, Banamex impersonation emails
- **Public Wi-Fi risks:** OXXO and mall Wi-Fi heavily targeted
- **Pesos/USD payments:** Most VPN providers accept international cards or PayPal

- **FastestVPN** most consistent across Mexican cities including Cancún and GDL
