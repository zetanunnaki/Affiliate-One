## Morocco: Gateway to Africa for Digital Nomads

Morocco is increasingly popular with European digital nomads thanks to its proximity, affordable living, and unique culture. Marrakech, Essaouira, and Tangier have growing remote work communities. However, VoIP restrictions make a VPN practically essential.

## VoIP Restrictions

Morocco restricts VoIP services including WhatsApp calls, Skype, and FaceTime. Maroc Telecom, Orange, and Inwi — the three main carriers — block these services to protect their voice call revenue. A VPN is the standard workaround used by millions of Moroccans and visitors.

## The Digital Nomad Scene

- **Marrakech:** Riads with Wi-Fi, growing co-working scene, affordable living
- **Essaouira:** Beach town popular with creative nomads, decent connectivity
- **Tangier:** Mediterranean port city 30 minutes from Spain by ferry, improving infrastructure
- **Casablanca:** Business capital with best broadband infrastructure
