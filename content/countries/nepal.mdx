## Nepal: Himalayas and Hashtags

Nepal offers a unique remote work experience — work from Kathmandu or Pokhara with Himalayan views, then disconnect completely on trekking routes. The government has occasionally restricted social media (TikTok banned 2023-2024), making VPN awareness high.

## Connectivity by Region

- **Kathmandu Valley:** Best internet. Nepal Telecom and Ncell provide decent 4G. Fiber available in central areas (20-50 Mbps)
- **Pokhara:** Lakeside tourist area has good café Wi-Fi. Speeds lower than Kathmandu
- **Trekking routes (Everest, Annapurna):** Very limited connectivity. Some teahouses have satellite Wi-Fi ($2-5/hour). Don't rely on internet above 3,000m
- **Lumbini and Terai:** Basic connectivity in towns. Rural areas limited

## Our Testing from Nepal

- **NordVPN** most reliable from Kathmandu with 400 Mbps on nearest servers (India/Singapore)
- **FastestVPN** best for maintaining VoIP calls across Nepal's variable networks
- **Surfshark** ultra-affordable — matching Nepal's budget-friendly living costs
- Download everything offline before trekking. Mountain areas have zero connectivity
