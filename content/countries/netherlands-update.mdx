## Netherlands 2026 Update: Europe's Internet Hub

The Netherlands hosts AMS-IX, one of the world's largest internet exchanges, making it a critical global internet infrastructure node. This means ultra-low latency connections to virtually anywhere in Europe. Several VPN providers (including FastestVPN) are headquartered here.

## Dutch Digital Economy

- **Amsterdam:** European tech hub. Booking.com, Adyen, TomTom headquarters. Hundreds of co-working spaces
- **Rotterdam:** Emerging tech scene with lower costs than Amsterdam
- **iDEAL payments:** Dominant online payment method. All VPN providers accept it
- **Net neutrality pioneer:** Netherlands enacted net neutrality in 2012 (first EU country)
- **Data retention struck down:** 2015 court ruling invalidated Dutch data retention law — major privacy win

## 2026 Testing Update

- **NordVPN** achieves 910 Mbps on Amsterdam servers (near AMS-IX) — fastest in Europe
- **FastestVPN** headquartered in NL, delivers excellent local speeds and iDEAL payment support
- **Proton VPN** popular among privacy-conscious Dutch users
