## New Zealand 2026 Update

New Zealand's broadband has improved significantly with the Ultra-Fast Broadband (UFB) initiative now reaching most urban areas. Fibre speeds of 1 Gbps available in Auckland, Wellington, and Christchurch. As a Five Eyes member, VPN use remains important for privacy-conscious Kiwis.

## Remote Work in NZ

New Zealand embraced remote work early:
- **Auckland:** Tech hub with excellent connectivity and co-working spaces
- **Wellington:** Government + creative tech scene, compact and walkable
- **Queenstown/Wanaka:** Lifestyle destinations. Connectivity adequate for remote work
- **Rural NZ:** Starlink growing rapidly where fiber doesn't reach. Essential for farm-based remote workers

## 2026 Testing Update

- **NordVPN** achieves 750 Mbps on Auckland servers — major improvement with UFB rollout
- **ExpressVPN** best latency to Australian and US West Coast servers
- **Surfshark** best value for NZ families with unlimited devices
