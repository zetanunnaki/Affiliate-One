## North Macedonia: VPN Guide

North Macedonia offers affordable internet and growing IT outsourcing sector in Skopje. Lake Ohrid is a UNESCO site with tourist-grade Wi-Fi. Competitive pricing and improving fiber deployment.

## Why Use a VPN in North Macedonia

A VPN is valuable in North Macedonia for:
- **Public Wi-Fi protection** — Hotels, cafés, and airports use shared networks
- **ISP privacy** — Prevent your internet provider from logging your browsing activity
- **Content access** — Access home-country streaming and services while abroad
- **Remote work security** — Encrypt work traffic on any network
