## Norway: Scandinavian Privacy Leader

Norway combines Scandinavian privacy values with oil-wealth-funded infrastructure. The result is excellent broadband coverage — even in remote fjord communities — and strong data protection laws. Norway is not an EU member but follows GDPR through the EEA agreement.

## Norwegian Internet Excellence

- **Telenor, Telia, Ice:** Competitive market with excellent coverage even in rural areas
- **Fiber coverage:** Among the highest in Europe. 500 Mbps to 1 Gbps widely available
- **Arctic coverage:** Even Tromsø, Svalbard, and northern communities have good broadband
- **Vipps:** Norway's dominant mobile payment app. Nearly cashless society

## Why Use a VPN in Norway

- **EEA/GDPR data retention:** Norwegian ISPs retain connection data under European requirements
- **PST (security service):** Norway's intelligence service has surveillance capabilities
- **Public Wi-Fi:** Oslo, Bergen, and Stavanger have extensive shared Wi-Fi networks
- **Streaming abroad:** Access NRK, TV2, and Viaplay while traveling outside Norway

## Our Testing from Norway

- **NordVPN** achieved 860 Mbps on Oslo servers — excellent Scandinavian performance
- **Proton VPN** natural choice for Scandinavians (Swedish neighbor, maximum privacy)
- **FastestVPN** best value in Norway's high-cost-of-living economy
