## Pakistan: Growing Restrictions, Growing VPN Use

Pakistan's internet landscape has become increasingly restrictive. The government has blocked social media platforms, imposed internet shutdowns during political events, and attempted to block VPN services. Despite these challenges, VPN use has surged — Pakistan consistently ranks among the top countries for VPN downloads.

## What Gets Restricted

- **Social media:** Twitter/X, Facebook, YouTube have all been temporarily blocked during political events
- **Wikipedia:** Blocked from 2023 over content disputes
- **TikTok:** Repeatedly banned and unbanned
- **VPN services:** PTA (Pakistan Telecommunication Authority) has attempted to block VPN apps, with partial success
- **Internet shutdowns:** Complete internet blackouts have occurred during elections and protests

## VPN Adoption

Pakistan has one of the highest VPN adoption rates in South Asia:
- Millions downloaded VPNs during social media blocks
- VPN awareness is high even among non-technical users
- The government's attempts to block VPNs have been partially effective but most premium services still work
- Configure VPN before traveling to Pakistan as some provider websites may be blocked
