## Papua New Guinea: VPN Guide

Papua New Guinea has very limited internet. Port Moresby has basic coverage. Highlands and remote areas are completely offline. One of the least connected Pacific nations.

## Why Use a VPN in Papua New Guinea

- **Public Wi-Fi protection** — Encrypt traffic on shared networks
- **ISP privacy** — Prevent browsing activity monitoring
- **Content access** — Access home-country services while abroad
- **Remote work security** — Protect work data on any network
