## Peru: Inca Trails and Internet Access

Peru offers remote workers a unique combination of cultural richness and growing digital infrastructure. Lima has a thriving tech and startup scene, while Cusco's tourist economy provides decent connectivity for nomads who want to work with Machu Picchu on the horizon.

## Internet by Region

- **Lima:** Best infrastructure. Movistar and Claro offer fiber in major neighborhoods (Miraflores, Barranco, San Isidro). 100-300 Mbps available
- **Cusco:** Tourist-grade Wi-Fi in the historic center. Good café and hotel Wi-Fi. Speeds lower than Lima (20-50 Mbps)
- **Sacred Valley:** Variable. Ollantaytambo and Urubamba have basic connectivity. Remote areas have none
- **Amazon (Iquitos):** Very limited internet. Satellite connections in some lodges

## Yape and Payment

Peru's Yape instant payment system has become dominant for local transactions. International VPN subscriptions require credit cards or PayPal, which work fine for online purchases.
