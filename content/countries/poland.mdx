## Poland: Eastern Europe's Tech Powerhouse

Poland has one of Europe's fastest-growing tech sectors, with Warsaw, Krakow, and Wroclaw hosting major development centers for Google, Microsoft, Amazon, and hundreds of tech companies. The combination of fast internet, affordable cost of living, and a skilled tech workforce makes Poland attractive for remote workers.

## Polish Internet Infrastructure

Poland's internet infrastructure is surprisingly competitive:
- **Orange, Play, T-Mobile, Plus:** Competitive market keeps prices low and speeds high
- **Fiber deployment:** Rapidly expanding, with 500 Mbps+ plans available in most cities for ~80-120 PLN/month ($20-30)
- **Mobile:** Excellent 4G/5G coverage from all carriers. Prepaid SIM data is very affordable
- **BLIK:** Poland's instant payment system is dominant for online payments — fast, free, and ubiquitous

## Remote Work in Poland

- **Warsaw:** The capital has a massive co-working ecosystem and is the business hub. International feel with widespread English
- **Krakow:** Beautiful historic city with growing tech scene and affordable living. Popular with expats
- **Wroclaw:** "The Polish Silicon Valley" — major tech hub with excellent quality of life
- **Gdansk/Sopot/Gdynia (Tri-City):** Coastal tech hub growing rapidly
