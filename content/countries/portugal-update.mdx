## Portugal 2026 Update: The Undisputed Nomad Capital of Europe

Portugal has solidified its position as Europe's #1 digital nomad destination. The D7 visa for remote workers, excellent weather, affordable living (outside Lisbon center), and mature co-working infrastructure make it unbeatable.

## The Lisbon Premium

Lisbon has become expensive relative to other Portuguese cities:
- **Lisbon:** Still the primary hub but rents have risen 40%+ since 2020
- **Porto:** Growing rapidly as a Lisbon alternative. Lower costs, strong tech scene
- **Algarve:** Beach-based remote work. Faro and Lagos have growing nomad communities
- **Madeira:** Ponta do Sol digital nomad village continues to attract remote workers
- **Azores:** Emerging as the next frontier for adventurous nomads
- **Ericeira:** Surf + work community north of Lisbon

## Infrastructure Maturity

Portugal's digital infrastructure is now among Europe's best:
- **MEO, NOS, Vodafone:** Fiber speeds up to 10 Gbps in cities for €30-50/month
- **MB Way:** Ubiquitous mobile payments
- **Co-working:** Hundreds of spaces across Lisbon, Porto, and smaller cities
- **GDPR enforcement:** CNPD actively enforcing privacy regulations
