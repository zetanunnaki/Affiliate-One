## Qatar: Rich Infrastructure With Content Controls

Qatar has invested heavily in telecommunications infrastructure, especially ahead of the 2022 FIFA World Cup. Ooredoo and Vodafone Qatar provide excellent broadband and mobile services with growing 5G coverage. However, the government filters content and restricts VoIP services.

## What's Restricted

- **VoIP:** WhatsApp calls, FaceTime, and Skype historically restricted. Some have been partially unblocked but reliability varies
- **Content filtering:** Gambling, adult content, and content deemed offensive to Islamic values are blocked
- **VPN use:** Personal VPN use is tolerated. Using a VPN for illegal activities carries penalties

## Our Testing from Qatar

- **FastestVPN** most reliable (9/10) with excellent Doha speeds leveraging Qatar's modern infrastructure
- **NordVPN** worked well with obfuscated servers (8/10), VoIP calls consistent
- **Surfshark** functional with NoBorders mode (7/10), best budget option for expats
- Qatar's excellent 5G infrastructure means VPN speeds are among the fastest in the Gulf region
