## Romania: Europe's Best-Kept Internet Secret

Romania has some of the fastest and cheapest internet in the world. Thanks to Digi (RCS & RDS), Romania consistently ranks in the top 10 globally for average internet speeds. Bucharest and Cluj-Napoca are emerging tech hubs with vibrant startup ecosystems.

Average broadband speeds exceed 200 Mbps, with gigabit connections widely available for under €10/month — a fraction of what similar speeds cost in Western Europe.

## Why Use a VPN in Romania

Despite excellent infrastructure, a VPN remains valuable:
- **EU data retention:** Romanian ISPs are subject to EU requirements
- **Privacy:** Romania's intelligence service (SRI) has surveillance capabilities
- **Public Wi-Fi:** Bucharest and Cluj have extensive café Wi-Fi networks — all shared
- **Content access:** Access Romanian services (Pro TV, Antena) while abroad
