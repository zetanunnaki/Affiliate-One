## Russia: Escalating Internet Restrictions

Russia's internet landscape has changed dramatically in recent years. The Sovereign Internet Law (2019), expanded blocking of VPN services, and the post-2022 acceleration of censorship have made Russia one of the most restrictive internet environments outside of China.

## What's Blocked in Russia

Russia's internet censorship agency Roskomnadzor maintains an extensive blocklist:
- **Social media:** Instagram, Facebook blocked since 2022. Twitter/X heavily throttled
- **News:** Many independent and international news outlets blocked
- **VPN services:** Numerous VPN providers officially banned. Provider websites blocked
- **Communication:** Some messaging platforms restricted

The blocking is enforced through ISP-level deep packet inspection (DPI) similar to China's Great Firewall, though not as comprehensive.

## VPN Legality in Russia

The situation is nuanced:
- **Using a VPN** is not explicitly illegal for individuals
- **Providing VPN services** that don't comply with Russian demands for access is illegal
- **Accessing blocked content** via VPN is technically illegal but enforcement targets providers, not individuals
- Many Russian citizens and businesses use VPNs daily

**Important:** We provide this information for educational purposes. The legal situation in Russia is complex and evolving. Research current regulations carefully.

## Essential: Prepare Before Arrival

If you're traveling to Russia, VPN preparation is critical:
1. Download multiple VPN apps before entering Russia (provider websites may be blocked)
2. Configure obfuscation/stealth mode
3. Download manual VPN configuration files as backup
4. Have at least 2 different VPN providers configured
5. Consider Shadowsocks or other censorship circumvention tools as backup

## Our Testing for Russia

VPN reliability in Russia is lower than in open-internet countries:
- **FastestVPN** was the most reliable at 6/10 connection success rate
- **NordVPN** obfuscated servers worked intermittently, 5/10 reliability
- **Proton VPN** Stealth protocol showed promise but inconsistent
- Speeds when connected are typically 50-200 Mbps depending on route and protocol
- Connection success varies by time of day and current enforcement intensity
