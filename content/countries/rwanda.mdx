## Rwanda: Africa's Singapore

Rwanda has positioned itself as Africa's most tech-forward nation. Kigali is clean, connected, and increasingly digital. The government has invested heavily in ICT infrastructure, attracted tech conferences (Africa's biggest tech events), and launched ambitious programs like drone delivery (Zipline) and digital governance.

## Digital Infrastructure

- **MTN, Airtel:** Main carriers with expanding 4G coverage
- **Fiber:** Growing in Kigali with government-backed broadband initiatives
- **iRembo:** Rwanda's digital government platform — most services available online
- **Kigali Innovation City:** Planned tech hub modeled on Silicon Valley
- **Cost:** Mobile data increasingly affordable. Government subsidizes digital access

## Internet Freedom

Rwanda's internet is more open than many African nations, but:
- **Government monitoring:** The government monitors online content
- **Content restrictions:** Some opposition media blocked
- **VPN use:** Legal and used by tech community and expats for privacy
