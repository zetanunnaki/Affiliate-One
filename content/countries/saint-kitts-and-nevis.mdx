## Internet Freedom & Privacy Landscape

Saint Kitts and Nevis has not been assessed in Freedom House's Freedom on the Net report, but as a stable Caribbean democracy, the twin-island federation maintains an open and unrestricted internet environment. The primary ISPs are Flow (owned by Liberty Latin America) and Digicel, offering 4G LTE mobile coverage and fiber-to-the-home broadband in Basseterre and major population centers. Internet penetration is approximately 75%, with both islands generally well-covered by mobile networks. The country's Citizenship by Investment Programme attracts international business people and digital nomads who require reliable, secure connectivity.

## VPN Legality

VPN use is completely legal in Saint Kitts and Nevis. There are no laws or regulations restricting the use of VPN technology, and the government does not interfere with encrypted communications. Both citizens and visitors can use VPNs freely.
