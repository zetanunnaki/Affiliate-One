## Saudi Arabia: Modernizing With Content Controls

Saudi Arabia has invested heavily in digital infrastructure as part of Vision 2030. Internet speeds are excellent, 5G coverage is expanding rapidly, and the tech sector is growing. However, the government maintains content filtering and VoIP restrictions.

## What's Restricted

- **VoIP services:** WhatsApp calls and FaceTime historically restricted, though some have been partially unblocked
- **Content filtering:** The CITC (Communications and IT Commission) filters gambling, adult, political, and religious content
- **Social media:** Generally accessible but monitored. Content critical of the government or religion may be removed
- **VPN websites:** Some VPN provider websites are blocked, though VPN apps typically work

## VPN Usage

VPN use for personal lawful purposes is widely tolerated in Saudi Arabia. Millions of residents and expats use VPNs daily for VoIP calls and accessing restricted content. The government primarily targets VPN providers rather than individual users.
