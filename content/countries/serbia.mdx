## Serbia: The Balkans' Tech Hub

Belgrade has emerged as one of the Balkans' most important tech cities. A strong software development tradition, affordable living, and competitive internet speeds make Serbia attractive for remote workers and outsourcing companies.

## Serbian Digital Infrastructure

- **SBB, MTS, Yettel:** Competitive ISP market with fiber expanding in Belgrade and Novi Sad
- **Speeds:** 100-500 Mbps fiber widely available in Belgrade for under €20/month — excellent value
- **Belgrade:** Growing co-working scene with spaces like Nova Iskra, Impact Hub, and Smart Office
- **Novi Sad:** Serbia's second city with a strong tech community (EXIT Festival also brings temporary infrastructure)

## Why Use a VPN in Serbia

- **No significant censorship:** Serbia has relatively open internet
- **Privacy:** Serbian ISPs can monitor traffic. A VPN encrypts your connection
- **Public Wi-Fi:** Belgrade's cafés and kafanas have free Wi-Fi — shared and unencrypted
- **Content access:** Access Serbian content (RTS, N1) while traveling abroad

## Our Testing from Serbia

- **NordVPN** achieved 820 Mbps on Belgrade servers — excellent for the region
- **FastestVPN** best value for Serbia's affordable cost of living
- **Proton VPN** popular among Serbia's privacy-conscious tech community
