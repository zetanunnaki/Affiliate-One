## Sierra Leone: VPN Guide

Sierra Leone has growing mobile internet primarily in Freetown. English-speaking. Government has restricted internet during elections. Beach areas may have limited connectivity.

## Why Use a VPN in Sierra Leone

- **Public Wi-Fi protection** — Encrypt traffic on shared networks
- **ISP privacy** — Prevent monitoring of your browsing activity
- **Content access** — Access home-country services while in Sierra Leone
- **Communication security** — Protect messaging and VoIP calls
