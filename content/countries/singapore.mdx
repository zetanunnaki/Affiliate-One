## Singapore: Business Hub With Content Controls

Singapore has world-class internet infrastructure — some of the fastest speeds in Asia with near-universal broadband coverage. However, the government maintains content regulations under the Broadcasting Act and POFMA (Protection from Online Falsehoods and Manipulation Act).

## Why Use a VPN in Singapore

- **Business security:** Singapore is a major financial and business hub. VPNs protect sensitive business communications on shared office networks
- **Content regulations:** Some content categories are blocked by default. While the blocking is less extensive than in China or the UAE, it exists
- **Regional travel:** Singapore is a gateway to Southeast Asia. A VPN configured here works well when traveling to more restrictive neighbors like Malaysia, Indonesia, or Vietnam
- **Wireless@SG:** Singapore's free nationwide Wi-Fi is convenient but shared among millions of users

## Singapore as a VPN Server Hub

Singapore hosts VPN servers for most major providers because of its excellent connectivity to all of Asia. When other Asian countries have local issues, Singapore servers are often the best alternative.

- **NordVPN** achieved 880 Mbps on local servers — the fastest speeds we measured anywhere in Asia
- **FastestVPN** had the best coverage for regional travel (reliable in neighboring countries)
- **FastestVPN** popular with Singapore's price-conscious consumers, offering excellent local speeds
