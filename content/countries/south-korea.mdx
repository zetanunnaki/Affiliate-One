## South Korea: The World's Fastest Internet

South Korea consistently ranks at or near the top of global internet speed rankings. With average speeds exceeding 200 Mbps and widespread 5G coverage, it's a paradise for bandwidth-intensive remote work. The country's tech-forward culture means excellent digital infrastructure everywhere.

## Why Use a VPN in South Korea

- **Content filtering:** The Korea Communications Standards Commission blocks certain categories of content. Some gambling, adult, and North Korea-related sites are blocked
- **Gaming:** South Korea's massive gaming industry means VPN use for accessing game servers in other regions is common. Lower latency to Japanese or US servers can be achieved with specific VPN servers
- **Privacy:** South Korea has a real-name verification system for many online services. A VPN adds privacy for browsing
- **Regional access:** Access Korean streaming (Wavve, Tving) and banking while abroad

## Korea's Digital Infrastructure

- **KT, SK Broadband, LG U+:** The big three provide fiber-to-the-home with speeds up to 10 Gbps in some areas
- **5G coverage:** South Korea was one of the first countries to deploy 5G nationwide. Coverage in Seoul is essentially complete
- **Café culture:** PC bangs (internet cafés) and regular cafés offer excellent Wi-Fi. Seoul is saturated with free Wi-Fi options
- **Public transport:** Seoul Metro and KTX trains offer Wi-Fi, though quality varies

## Our Testing from South Korea

- **NordVPN** achieved 860 Mbps on Seoul servers — near the top of our global rankings
- **ExpressVPN** offered the lowest latency to Japanese gaming servers (under 20ms)
- **Surfshark** best value for Korean families with multiple devices
