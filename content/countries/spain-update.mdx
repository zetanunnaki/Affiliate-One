## Spain 2026 Update: Nomad Visa Driving Growth

Spain's digital nomad visa (Law 28/2022) has been a major success, attracting thousands of international remote workers. Combined with the Beckham Law tax benefits, Spain is now Europe's second most popular nomad destination after Portugal.

## Hotspot Updates

- **Barcelona:** Still the primary tech hub but expensive. Gracia, Poble Sec offer more affordable co-working
- **Valencia:** Rising fast. Lower costs than Barcelona, excellent quality of life, growing tech scene
- **Las Palmas (Gran Canaria):** Year-round warm weather, strong nomad community, excellent internet
- **Málaga:** Growing tech hub with Google choosing it for a cybersecurity center. "Silicon Beach" nickname
- **Tenerife:** South Tenerife attracts surfer-nomads. Norte has faster fiber
