## Spain: Sun, Siestas, and Strong Internet

Spain has become one of Europe's top digital nomad destinations, driven by its digital nomad visa (introduced 2023), affordable cost of living outside major cities, and excellent quality of life. Barcelona, Valencia, the Canary Islands, and Malaga are global remote work hotspots.

## The Spanish Digital Nomad Visa

Spain's Law 28/2022 created a specific visa for remote workers. Key requirements include working for a non-Spanish company and earning above the Spanish minimum wage. The visa is valid for up to 5 years and offers tax advantages (Beckham Law) for the first 6 years.

This has attracted thousands of international remote workers, all of whom need secure internet connections.

## Internet Infrastructure

- **Movistar, Vodafone, Orange, MasMovil:** Competitive fiber market with speeds up to 1 Gbps widely available in cities for €30-50/month
- **Canary Islands:** Excellent infrastructure for an island region. Las Palmas de Gran Canaria has some of Spain's best co-working spaces
- **Barcelona:** World-class digital infrastructure, hundreds of co-working spaces, major tech hub
- **Valencia:** Growing tech scene with more affordable pricing than Barcelona

## Our Testing from Spain

- **NordVPN** delivered 830 Mbps on Madrid servers with excellent performance to European and US destinations
- **Surfshark** best value for nomads doing extended stays — unlimited devices for all gear
- **ExpressVPN** most consistent across mainland Spain, Balearic, and Canary Islands
