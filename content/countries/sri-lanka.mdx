## Sri Lanka: Digital Recovery

Sri Lanka's internet landscape was heavily impacted by the 2022 economic crisis, during which the government blocked social media and VPN downloads surged. The country is recovering, but VPN awareness remains high — and useful, given occasional content restrictions.

## Post-Crisis Internet

- **Dialog, Mobitel, Hutch:** Major carriers rebuilding and expanding coverage
- **Colombo:** Best connectivity. Fiber expanding with speeds up to 100 Mbps
- **Southern coast (Galle, Mirissa):** Tourist-grade Wi-Fi in hotels and cafés. Growing nomad community
- **Central highlands (Kandy, Ella):** Basic connectivity in towns, limited in tea country
- **Affordable:** SIM data very cheap. $5-10 for generous monthly data packages
