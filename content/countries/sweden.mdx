## Sweden: Where Proton VPN Was Born

Sweden holds a special place in VPN history as the home of Proton VPN VPN, one of the most privacy-focused VPN providers in the world. The country combines excellent digital infrastructure with strong privacy traditions, though it is a member of the Fourteen Eyes intelligence alliance.

## Swedish Privacy Context

Sweden has a complex relationship with privacy. On one hand, it has strong constitutional protections for freedom of speech and press. On the other, the FRA (National Defence Radio Establishment) has broad signals intelligence capabilities, and Sweden participates in European intelligence sharing.

The 2023 police raid on Proton VPN's office — which found no customer data because Proton VPN doesn't store any — demonstrated both the risks and the effectiveness of proper no-logs VPN architecture.

## Why Use a VPN in Sweden

- **FRA surveillance:** Sweden's signals intelligence agency monitors cross-border communications
- **ISP data retention:** Swedish ISPs are subject to EU data retention requirements
- **Nearly cashless society:** Sweden's digital payments ecosystem means more traceable transactions — a VPN adds privacy to your online financial activity
- **Excellent infrastructure:** Sweden's fast internet means VPN overhead is minimal
