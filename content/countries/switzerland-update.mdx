## Switzerland 2026 Update: revDSG in Effect

Switzerland's revised Federal Act on Data Protection (revDSG/nFADP) has been in effect since September 2023, bringing Swiss data protection closer to GDPR standards while maintaining Switzerland's unique privacy traditions. The FDPIC (Federal Data Protection and Information Commissioner) has enhanced enforcement powers.

Switzerland's privacy technology ecosystem continues to grow:
