## Syria: VPN Guide

Syria's internet is severely damaged by conflict. Government monitors traffic extensively. VPN essential but reliability varies by region and controlling authority. Active conflict zones may have no telecommunications.

## Why Use a VPN in Syria

- **Public Wi-Fi protection** — Encrypt traffic on shared networks
- **ISP privacy** — Prevent monitoring of your browsing activity
- **Content access** — Access home-country services while in Syria
- **Communication security** — Protect messaging and VoIP calls
