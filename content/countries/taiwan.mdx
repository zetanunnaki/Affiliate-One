## Taiwan: Free Internet, Fast Speeds

Taiwan stands out in Asia for its completely open and uncensored internet. There are no government restrictions on content, no VPN blocking, and no Great Firewall. Combined with excellent infrastructure (widespread fiber, extensive free Wi-Fi), Taiwan is ideal for remote work.

## Why Use a VPN in Taiwan

Despite the open internet, a VPN is still valuable:
- **Public Wi-Fi security:** The iTaiwan free Wi-Fi network covers thousands of locations but is shared and unencrypted
- **Content access:** Access services from your home country while in Taiwan
- **ISP privacy:** Taiwanese ISPs (Chunghwa Telecom, FarEasTone) can monitor browsing activity
- **Travel to China:** If you cross to mainland China, your VPN must already be configured. Taiwan is the perfect place to set up before a China trip

## Digital Infrastructure

Taiwan has excellent connectivity:
- **Chunghwa Telecom, FarEasTone, Taiwan Mobile:** Competitive providers with fiber widely available
- **iTaiwan:** Free government Wi-Fi available in MRT stations, tourist spots, convenience stores
- **Speeds:** Average exceeds 150 Mbps. Gigabit connections available in Taipei for ~$25/month
- **7-Eleven and FamilyMart:** Over 10,000 convenience stores with Wi-Fi
