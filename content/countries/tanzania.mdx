## Tanzania: Safari Country with Social Media Levies

Tanzania imposed social media taxes and online content regulations that have driven VPN adoption. The government requires bloggers and online content creators to register and pay fees, and has restricted social media during political events.

## Content Restrictions

- **Social media levy:** Users taxed for accessing social media platforms
- **Blogging registration:** Online content creators must register with the government
- **Content regulations:** Government can order removal of online content
- **VPN use:** Legal and widespread as a response to social media taxes

## Connectivity by Region

- **Dar es Salaam:** Best infrastructure. Vodacom, Airtel, Tigo provide good 4G coverage
- **Zanzibar:** Tourist areas (Stone Town, beach resorts) have decent Wi-Fi. Growing digital nomad scene
- **Kilimanjaro:** No connectivity on the mountain. Download everything offline before trekking
- **Safari areas (Serengeti, Ngorongoro):** Luxury lodges may have satellite Wi-Fi. Budget camps typically don't
