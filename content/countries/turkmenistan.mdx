## Turkmenistan: VPN Guide

Turkmenistan has one of the world's most restricted internet environments. VPN use is technically illegal but practiced. Extremely slow and expensive internet. One of the least connected countries globally.

## Why Use a VPN in Turkmenistan

- **Public Wi-Fi protection** — Encrypt traffic on shared networks
- **ISP privacy** — Prevent monitoring of your browsing activity
- **Content access** — Access home-country services while in Turkmenistan
- **Communication security** — Protect messaging and VoIP calls
