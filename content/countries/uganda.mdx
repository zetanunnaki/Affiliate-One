## Uganda: Where VPN Use Became Mainstream

Uganda became a VPN adoption case study when the government imposed an OTT (Over-The-Top) social media tax in 2018, charging users daily fees to access WhatsApp, Facebook, and Twitter. The response was immediate and massive — VPN downloads surged as millions of Ugandans used VPNs to bypass the tax.

## The OTT Tax Legacy

The OTT tax was eventually replaced by a broader internet excise tax, but its impact on VPN awareness was permanent. Uganda now has one of the highest VPN awareness rates in Africa. During the 2021 election, the government imposed a complete internet shutdown, further cementing VPN as an essential tool.

## Internet Infrastructure

- **MTN, Airtel, Africell:** Major carriers with expanding 4G coverage
- **Kampala:** Best connectivity with fiber available in some areas
- **Mobile money:** MTN MoMo and Airtel Money dominant for payments
- **Safari areas:** Bwindi (gorilla trekking), Queen Elizabeth NP have limited connectivity
