## UK 2026 Update: Surveillance Laws Expand

The UK's surveillance landscape continues to expand:
- **Investigatory Powers Act (Snoopers' Charter):** ISPs still required to store 12 months of browsing history. 40+ agencies can access without warrant
- **Online Safety Act (2023):** Ofcom now has power to compel platforms to scan for content. Implications for E2E encryption remain unclear
- **Five Eyes:** UK is a core member, sharing intelligence with US, Canada, Australia, NZ
- **Digital identity:** Government pushing digital ID initiatives with privacy implications

## UK Remote Work Culture

The UK has one of Europe's highest remote work rates:
- **London:** World-class co-working (WeWork, Spaces, Second Home). Tube Wi-Fi at most stations
- **Manchester, Bristol, Edinburgh:** Growing tech hubs with lower costs than London
- **Pubs with Wi-Fi:** Britain's pubs increasingly serve as remote offices. Always use VPN
- **Trains:** National Rail Wi-Fi available but slow and shared. VPN essential
- **BT, Virgin Media, Sky:** Competitive broadband market with fiber expanding
