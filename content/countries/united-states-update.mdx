## US 2026 Update: Privacy Patchwork Grows

The US still lacks a comprehensive federal privacy law. Instead, the state-level patchwork continues to expand:
- **California CPRA:** Strongest state privacy law, with the California Privacy Protection Agency actively enforcing
- **Colorado, Connecticut, Virginia, Utah, Iowa, Tennessee, Montana, Texas, Oregon, Delaware, New Hampshire:** All have enacted privacy laws
- **No federal ISP privacy:** Your ISP can still sell your browsing data in most states
- **Five Eyes:** The US is the leading member of the intelligence-sharing alliance
