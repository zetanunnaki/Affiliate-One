## Uzbekistan: Opening Up But Still Restricted

Uzbekistan has been gradually opening its internet since President Mirziyoyev took office in 2016, but restrictions remain. Some websites are blocked, social media has been throttled, and the government monitors internet traffic. Tashkent, Samarkand, and Bukhara attract growing numbers of tourists and remote workers.

## Internet Reality

- **Ucell, Beeline, UzMobile:** Major carriers with expanding 4G coverage
- **Broadband:** Available in Tashkent and major cities. Speeds improving but still behind regional peers
- **Content blocking:** Some news sites, social media platforms periodically restricted
- **Surveillance:** Government monitors internet activity through state-controlled infrastructure

## Silk Road Tourism and Connectivity

Uzbekistan's tourism boom (Samarkand, Bukhara, Khiva) is driving infrastructure improvements:
- Tourist hotels generally have Wi-Fi
- Mobile coverage along the main Silk Road route is adequate
- Remote areas and desert regions have limited connectivity
