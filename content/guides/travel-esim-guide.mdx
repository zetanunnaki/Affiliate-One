## Why eSIMs Are a Game-Changer for Travelers

eSIMs (embedded SIMs) let you add a data plan for any country without buying a physical SIM card. You activate instantly by scanning a QR code — no airport SIM kiosks, no fumbling with tiny trays, no losing your home SIM.

For remote workers traveling internationally, eSIMs solve the connectivity problem within minutes of landing.

## eSIM + VPN: The Secure Travel Combo

An eSIM gives you connectivity. A VPN makes it secure:

1. **Before departure:** Install VPN + purchase eSIM data plan
2. **On arrival:** Activate eSIM (instant data), connect VPN
3. **Benefit:** Private, encrypted mobile data in any country

This combo is especially important in countries with internet restrictions (China, UAE, Turkey). With your own eSIM data + VPN, you bypass hotel Wi-Fi security concerns entirely.

## Security Considerations

- **eSIM data goes through local carrier networks** — subject to local monitoring and restrictions. Use a VPN
- **Your home SIM stays safe** — no risk of SIM swap in foreign country
- **Keep your home number for 2FA** — don't rely on eSIM number for authentication
- **Multiple eSIMs:** iPhone and newer Android phones support multiple eSIMs. Use different ones for different regions

## Setup (5 Minutes)

1. Check your phone supports eSIM (most phones from 2020+)
2. Purchase a plan from Airalo, Holafly, or your preferred provider
3. Scan the QR code or use the provider's app to install
4. In Settings > Cellular/Mobile > select your eSIM for data
5. Keep your home SIM active for calls/texts and 2FA
6. Connect your VPN — all eSIM traffic is now encrypted
