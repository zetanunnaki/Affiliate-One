## VPN on Android: The Best Mobile Platform for VPN

Android is the best mobile platform for VPN users. Unlike iOS, Android supports true split tunneling, always-on VPN at the system level, and the ability to block all non-VPN traffic natively. Every major VPN provider offers a polished Android app.

## The Android Advantage

What Android offers that iOS doesn't:
- **System-level always-on VPN:** Settings > Network > VPN > gear icon > Always-on VPN
- **Block non-VPN connections:** Same menu — blocks ALL traffic without VPN
- **Split tunneling:** Choose which apps go through VPN vs direct
- **Work Profile:** Separate VPN for work and personal apps
- **Sideloading:** Install VPN APKs directly if Play Store is blocked in your country

## Setup (5 Minutes)

1. Download your VPN app from Google Play Store
2. Log in with your credentials
3. Tap Connect — you're protected
4. Go to Settings > Network > VPN > tap gear icon next to your VPN
5. Enable "Always-on VPN" and "Block connections without VPN"
6. In the VPN app: enable auto-connect, set protocol to WireGuard

## Android-Specific Settings

- **Battery optimization:** Exclude your VPN app from battery optimization (Settings > Apps > VPN > Battery > Unrestricted) to prevent Android from killing it in the background
- **Private DNS:** Android supports DNS-over-TLS natively (Settings > Network > Private DNS). Set to "Automatic" or a provider like dns.google or 1dot1dot1dot1.cloudflare-dns.com
- **Split tunneling:** In your VPN app, exclude banking apps that flag VPN connections
