## Android: The Best Mobile VPN Platform

Android is the best mobile platform for VPN users — period. System-level always-on VPN, true split tunneling, the ability to block non-VPN traffic, and Work Profile separation. No other mobile OS offers this level of VPN integration.

## Our Rankings

### 1. NordVPN — Best Android VPN
NordLynx (WireGuard) delivers the fastest speeds with minimal battery impact. Split tunneling, Threat Protection (blocks malicious apps), Meshnet, Dark Web Monitor. The most feature-rich Android VPN.

### 2. Surfshark — Best Value for Android
Unlimited devices — your Android phone, tablet, TV box, and family's devices all on one subscription. CleanWeb blocks ads. GPS spoofing (Bypasser) unique to Android. $2.29/month.

### 3. FastestVPN — Most Polished Android App
Beautiful Material Design. Lightway protocol for instant connections. Automatic best-server selection. The smoothest Android VPN experience.
