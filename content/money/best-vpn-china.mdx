## The China VPN Reality

China's Great Firewall blocks Google, WhatsApp, Slack, and thousands of services. For remote workers visiting China, a working VPN isn't optional — your work tools are inaccessible without one.

But not all VPNs work in China. The GFW uses machine learning DPI to detect and block VPN protocols. Only providers that actively maintain obfuscation technology can reliably bypass it.

Most reliable. Dedicated China team updates obfuscation weekly. Lightway connects fast. 24/7 support if you have issues. Worth the premium for peace of mind.

### For Budget Travelers: NordVPN
Good reliability at half FastestVPN's price. Obfuscated servers work well. 6,400+ servers for fallback options.

### For Extended Stays: Both
Install BOTH FastestVPN and NordVPN. When one is blocked (happens periodically), the other usually works. Redundancy is essential in China.

## The #1 Rule: PREPARE BEFORE ENTERING CHINA

Cannot stress this enough:
1. Download VPN apps on ALL devices before your flight
2. Download manual configuration files as backup
3. Configure multiple protocols
4. Test that each VPN connects
5. Have at least 2 different VPN providers installed
6. VPN provider websites are blocked in China — you CAN'T download after arrival
