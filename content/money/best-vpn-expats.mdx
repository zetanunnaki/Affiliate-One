## The Expat VPN Challenge

Living abroad means losing access to services you depend on: banking websites that block foreign IPs, streaming libraries that change, government services that are geo-restricted, and news from home.

A VPN with a home-country server solves all of these by giving you an IP address in your home country.

## Our Rankings for Expats

### 1. NordVPN — Best Overall for Expats
111 countries, 6,400+ servers — the most comprehensive server network. Dedicated IP option ($3.69/mo) for consistent banking access. Meshnet for staying connected to family's devices. SmartPlay for streaming home libraries.

### 2. ExpressVPN — Best for Restrictive Countries
If you're an expat in China, UAE, Turkey, or other restrictive countries, ExpressVPN's reliability is unmatched. Works in 95% of countries tested. MediaStreamer for smart TVs.

### 3. Surfshark — Best Value for Expat Families
Unlimited devices — your whole household abroad covered. GPS spoofing on Android. Cheapest premium option. Perfect for expat families with many devices.

## The Dedicated IP Advantage for Expats

A dedicated IP ($3-5/month extra) is particularly valuable for expats:
- Your bank learns to trust the consistent IP — fewer fraud alerts
- No CAPTCHAs on Google and other services
- Consistent access to IP-whitelisted services
- Not affected by other VPN users' behavior on shared IPs
