## iPhone VPN: What Apple Allows

iOS is more restrictive than Android for VPN functionality — no split tunneling, no system-level always-on. But within Apple's constraints, VPN apps on iPhone are polished, reliable, and battery-efficient.

## Our Rankings

### 1. ExpressVPN — Best iPhone VPN
The most polished iOS experience. Lightway protocol connects in under 1 second. On Demand rules work flawlessly. Native iOS design with home screen widget. Best for users who want it to "just work."

### 2. NordVPN — Most Features on iPhone
NordLynx for top speeds. Threat Protection blocks malicious sites in Safari. Dark Web Monitor. Meshnet. The most feature-rich iPhone VPN despite iOS limitations.

### 3. Surfshark — Best Value for iPhone
Your iPhone + iPad + Mac all covered on one subscription with unlimited devices. CleanWeb blocks Safari ads. $2.29/month — can't beat the value.
