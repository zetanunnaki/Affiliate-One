## Mac VPN: Apple Silicon Native Required

In 2026, all major VPN providers offer native Apple Silicon apps. Running a VPN through Rosetta translation is unnecessary and wastes battery. Choose a provider with a true native app.

## Our Rankings

### 1. NordVPN — Best Mac VPN Overall
The most feature-rich macOS app. NordLynx delivers near-gigabit speeds on M-series Macs. Split tunneling (finally on Mac!), Threat Protection, Meshnet, and menu bar quick-connect. The complete package.

### 2. FastestVPN — Most Polished Mac Experience
The most beautiful macOS VPN app. Follows Apple Human Interface Guidelines perfectly. Lightway protocol for instant connections. Network Lock kill switch. If design matters, this is it.

### 3. Surfshark — Best Mac Value
Unlimited devices — your MacBook, iMac, Mac Mini, and every other device on one subscription. CleanWeb blocks Safari ads. Split tunneling on Mac. Most affordable premium VPN.

## FileVault + VPN: Complete Mac Security

Enable both for comprehensive protection:
- **FileVault:** Encrypts your entire disk. If your Mac is stolen, data is unreadable
- **VPN:** Encrypts all network traffic. ISP can't see what you do online
- **Together:** Data protected at rest (FileVault) AND in transit (VPN)

Both are free (FileVault built-in, VPN ~$3/mo) and have negligible performance impact on M-series Macs.
