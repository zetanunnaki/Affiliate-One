## Why You Need a VPN for Netflix

Netflix offers different content libraries in every country. A show available in the US might not exist in the UK catalog, and vice versa. A VPN lets you connect to a server in another country and access that region's Netflix library — expanding your available content by thousands of titles.

But not every VPN works with Netflix. The streaming giant actively blocks VPN IP addresses, and most providers fail more often than they succeed. We tested four top VPNs from 15 countries over 30 days to find which ones deliver consistent, reliable Netflix access.

## How We Tested

Our testing methodology was rigorous and real-world:

- **15 countries tested:** US, UK, Canada, Japan, Australia, Germany, France, Brazil, India, South Korea, Netherlands, Italy, Spain, Sweden, and Turkey
- **4 VPN providers:** NordVPN, Surfshark, Proton VPN, FastestVPN
- **Test duration:** 30 consecutive days, testing each provider 3 times per country
- **Metrics tracked:** Connection success rate, stream quality (720p/1080p/4K), buffering events per hour, initial load time
- **Device coverage:** Windows laptop, Android phone, Apple TV, Fire TV Stick

A "pass" required streaming for 60+ minutes without interruption at 1080p or higher. A "fail" meant Netflix detected the VPN and showed the proxy error screen, or quality dropped below 720p consistently.

## FAQ

### Is using a VPN with Netflix legal?
Yes, using a VPN is legal in most countries. However, it may violate Netflix's Terms of Service. Netflix's response is typically to block the VPN connection — they don't ban accounts for VPN usage.

### Will Netflix cancel my account for using a VPN?
No. Netflix has never been known to cancel accounts for VPN use. At worst, you'll see the proxy error message and need to disconnect the VPN or switch servers.

### Why does Netflix block VPNs?
Content licensing. Netflix pays different rights holders in each country. When you use a VPN to access another country's library, you're viewing content Netflix hasn't licensed for your region. Studios pressure Netflix to enforce geographic restrictions.

### Can I use a free VPN for Netflix?
Free VPNs almost never work with Netflix. They have limited server IPs that get detected and blocked quickly. Free VPNs also tend to throttle speeds below what's needed for HD streaming. Even Proton VPN's free tier doesn't include streaming server access.

### What speed do I need for Netflix streaming?
Netflix recommends 5 Mbps for HD and 25 Mbps for 4K Ultra HD. All four VPNs we tested exceeded these thresholds on nearby servers. The real bottleneck is usually detection, not speed.
