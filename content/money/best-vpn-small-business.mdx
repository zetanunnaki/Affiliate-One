## Small Business VPN: What You Actually Need

Most small businesses don't need enterprise VPN solutions. A consumer VPN with team-friendly features covers 90% of use cases. Here's what actually matters and which providers deliver.

## Our Rankings

### 1. NordVPN Teams — Best for Managed Teams (5-25)
Centralized admin dashboard, add/remove users, dedicated IPs, Meshnet for virtual office networking. Priority support. The most complete business package.

**Key feature:** Meshnet creates an encrypted peer-to-peer network between team devices — like a virtual office LAN. Free for all NordVPN users.

### 2. Proton VPN Business — Best for Privacy-Sensitive Industries
Swiss jurisdiction, open-source, SSO integration, dedicated servers. Included in Proton for Business bundle with Mail, Drive, Calendar, and Pass. Ideal for legal, healthcare, and financial services.

**Key feature:** Entire Proton suite (VPN + encrypted email + encrypted storage + password manager) for one price.

### 3. FastestVPN — Best Budget for Tiny Teams (2-10)
Individual subscriptions at $2.29/user/month. Unlimited devices per person. No centralized management but the price can't be beat. Each team member manages their own VPN.

**Key feature:** Unlimited devices means no counting — every employee's laptop, phone, and tablet on one subscription.

## Deployment Checklist

1. Choose provider based on team size (see above)
2. Create team account or buy individual subscriptions
3. Distribute credentials/invites to all employees
4. Require installation on work laptop + phone
5. Mandate settings: kill switch ON, auto-connect ON, WireGuard
6. Document VPN policy in employee handbook
7. Include in onboarding process for new hires
8. Revoke access immediately when employees leave
