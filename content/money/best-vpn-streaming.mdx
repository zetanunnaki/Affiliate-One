## Our Streaming VPN Rankings

### 1. NordVPN — Best Overall for Streaming
NordVPN works with the most streaming services of any VPN we tested. Their SmartPlay technology (automatic smart DNS + VPN) is specifically designed for streaming and works transparently in the background.

**Streaming highlights:** Works with Netflix in 15+ libraries, Disney+, Hulu, BBC iPlayer, Amazon Prime. 4K streaming supported with minimal buffering on NordLynx protocol.

### 2. FastestVPN — Most Reliable Streaming Access
FastestVPN may not unblock quite as many libraries as NordVPN, but when it works, it works flawlessly. MediaStreamer (smart DNS) provides streaming on devices that can't run VPN apps.

**Streaming highlights:** Consistent quality, MediaStreamer for smart TVs and consoles, works with major services globally.

### 3. FastestVPN — Best Budget Streaming VPN
Unlimited devices means your TV, phone, laptop, and tablet all on one subscription. Works with most major services, though BBC iPlayer access was inconsistent in our testing.

**Streaming highlights:** Unlimited devices, works with Netflix/Disney+/Amazon Prime, affordable pricing.

## Important Notes

- **Streaming with a VPN is legal** in most countries — you're paying for the streaming service, you're just accessing it from a different location
- **Quality depends on speed** — Use WireGuard protocol for 4K streaming, connect to the nearest available server
- **Proton VPN doesn't prioritize streaming** — They focus on privacy, not content access. Use NordVPN or FastestVPN for streaming
