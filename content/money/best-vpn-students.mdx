## Why Students Need a VPN

University Wi-Fi is a security nightmare. Hundreds or thousands of students sharing the same network, with varying levels of technical hygiene. Plus, universities often restrict certain content (streaming, gaming, P2P) and monitor network usage.

A VPN protects you on campus Wi-Fi, lets you bypass network restrictions, and keeps your browsing private from the university IT department.

- **Campus Wi-Fi security** — Encrypt your traffic on shared university networks
