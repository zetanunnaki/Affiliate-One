## What Teams Need From a VPN

Individual VPN accounts work for freelancers, but teams need:
- **Centralized management** — Add/remove team members without sharing passwords
- **Dedicated IP** — For IP-whitelisted company firewalls and services
- **Team billing** — Single invoice for the whole team
- **Priority support** — Faster response times for business-critical issues
- **Compliance** — Audit trails and access controls

## Our Top Picks for Teams

### 1. NordVPN Teams — Best Overall for Teams

NordVPN's team plan offers centralized management, dedicated IPs, and Meshnet for virtual office networking. The admin dashboard lets you add/remove members, enforce kill switch usage, and monitor connection status.

**Team highlights:**
- Admin dashboard for user management
- Dedicated IP for each team member ($3.69/mo extra)
- Meshnet creates encrypted peer-to-peer LAN between team devices
- 10 devices per license
- Priority 24/7 support

**Pricing:** Contact sales for team pricing (typically $7-9/user/month)

### 2. Proton VPN Business — Best for Privacy-First Teams

Proton VPN Business is ideal for teams handling sensitive data — legal firms, healthcare, journalism, and financial services. Swiss jurisdiction plus open-source code provides the strongest privacy guarantees.

**Team highlights:**
- Swiss privacy laws protect team data
- Dedicated servers and IPs
- Secure Core routing for sensitive traffic
- SSO integration (SAML)
- Included in Proton for Business (with Mail, Drive, Calendar, Pass)

**Pricing:** $9.99/user/month (includes full Proton suite)

### 3. FastestVPN — Best Budget for Small Teams

For teams of 2-10 where budget matters and centralized management isn't critical, individual FastestVPN subscriptions offer the best value. Unlimited devices means each team member covers all their gear.

**Team highlights:**
- $2.29/user/month on 2-year plans
- Unlimited devices per subscription
- CleanWeb blocks threats across the team
- No centralized management (each user manages their own)

**Best for:** Tiny teams that don't need admin controls.

## When to Upgrade Beyond Consumer VPN

| Team Size | Recommendation |
|-----------|---------------|
| 1-5 | Individual VPN subscriptions (FastestVPN for value) |
| 5-15 | NordVPN Teams or Proton VPN Business |
| 15-50 | NordLayer or Perimeter 81 (enterprise ZTNA) |
| 50+ | Zscaler, Cloudflare Access, or Tailscale |

## The NordVPN Meshnet Advantage

For small teams, NordVPN's Meshnet is a game-changer. It creates an encrypted mesh network between up to 60 devices. Team members can:
- Share files as if they're on the same LAN
- Access shared printers and network drives
- Connect to a colleague's local development server
- All traffic between devices is encrypted peer-to-peer

Meshnet is **free** for all NordVPN users — even on the basic plan.
