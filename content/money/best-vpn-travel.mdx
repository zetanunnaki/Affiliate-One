## What Travel VPNs Need

Not all VPNs work well for travel. The best travel VPN needs to:

- **Work in restrictive countries** — China, UAE, Turkey, Russia, Egypt
- **Connect quickly** — Hotel and airport Wi-Fi often has captive portals
- **Handle network switches** — Moving between Wi-Fi and cellular shouldn't drop the connection
- **Have servers worldwide** — Nearby servers for speed, home servers for content
- **Offer obfuscation** — Bypass VPN detection on restrictive networks

## Pre-Travel VPN Checklist

1. Download and install VPN apps on ALL devices before departure
2. Configure multiple protocols (WireGuard + OpenVPN TCP as backup)
3. Enable obfuscation / stealth mode
4. Test the connection to servers in your destination country
5. Save manual configuration files as backup
6. Note your VPN provider's support contact info
7. If visiting China/UAE/Russia: have a second VPN provider as backup
