**Yes — NordVPN is worth it for most users.** At $3.39/month on the 2-year plan, it delivers the best combination of speed, security, and features we've tested. The only reasons to look elsewhere are if you need unlimited devices (Surfshark) or maximum privacy at the expense of everything else (Proton VPN).

## Where NordVPN Excels

### Speed
NordLynx (NordVPN's WireGuard implementation) retained 89% of our base speed on nearby servers — the fastest we've tested. For remote workers on video calls, cloud sync, and large file transfers, this headroom matters.

### Security
System-level kill switch using Windows Filtering Platform (not just app-level). DNS leak protection tested across 50 runs with zero leaks. AES-256-GCM encryption. Deloitte-audited no-logs infrastructure.

### Streaming
Worked with all 10 streaming services we tested — Netflix (15+ libraries), Disney+, BBC iPlayer, Hulu, Amazon Prime, HBO Max, Peacock, DAZN, and more. SmartPlay technology handles DNS routing automatically.

### Meshnet
Free peer-to-peer encrypted networking between your devices and your team's devices. Like a virtual office LAN. Up to 60 devices. This alone would cost $50-100/month as a standalone product.

### Threat Protection
Blocks malicious websites, trackers, and ads at the DNS level. Works even when the VPN tunnel is off. Caught 94% of test malware domains in our testing.

## Where NordVPN Falls Short

- **Price on monthly plans** — $12.99/month is expensive. The value only kicks in on annual or 2-year commitments.
- **10-device cap** — Large families or device-heavy users may hit the limit. Surfshark offers unlimited for $1.99/mo.
- **Split tunneling on iOS/macOS** — Not available on iOS (Apple restriction), recently added on macOS but still maturing.
- **No free tier** — Proton VPN offers a genuine free tier if you want to test before committing.
