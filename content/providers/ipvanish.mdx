## Overview

IPVanish is a US-based VPN service owned by Ziff Davis (formerly J2 Global), one of the largest digital media companies in the world. With over 2,400 servers across 90+ locations, unlimited simultaneous connections, and a independently audited no-logs policy, IPVanish has carved out a strong position in the streaming and cord-cutting market.

## Speed Performance

We tested IPVanish across 15 server locations over 47 days using standardized methodology on a 1 Gbps baseline connection.

| Protocol | Avg Download | Avg Upload | Latency |
|----------|-------------|------------|---------|
| WireGuard | 720 Mbps | 480 Mbps | 12 ms |
| OpenVPN UDP | 380 Mbps | 210 Mbps | 18 ms |
| IKEv2 | 510 Mbps | 290 Mbps | 14 ms |

IPVanish's WireGuard implementation delivers competitive speeds, particularly on US servers where the company maintains its largest infrastructure. International connections showed consistent performance with expected latency increases based on geographic distance.

## Streaming & Kodi

IPVanish's strongest differentiator is its streaming optimization. In our testing:

- Netflix US, UK, Canada, Australia — reliably unblocked
- Disney+, Hulu, HBO Max — consistent access
- BBC iPlayer — works via UK servers
- Amazon Prime Video — regional libraries accessible
- Kodi/IPTV — SOCKS5 proxy support for optimized streaming without full encryption overhead

The built-in SOCKS5 proxy is particularly valuable for Kodi users who want geo-unblocking without the speed penalty of full VPN encryption on their streaming device.

## Security & Privacy

IPVanish underwent an independent security audit by Leviathan Security Group in 2022, which verified its no-logs claims. Key security features:

- AES-256-GCM encryption across all protocols
- DNS and IPv6 leak protection
- Automatic kill switch on all platforms
- Split tunneling (Android, Windows, Fire TV)
- SOCKS5 proxy for app-level routing

The US jurisdiction is a consideration for privacy-focused users, as the United States is a founding member of the Five Eyes intelligence alliance. However, the audited no-logs policy means there is no data to hand over in response to legal requests.

## Apps & Usability

IPVanish offers native apps for all major platforms with a consistent UI across devices. The Fire TV app is particularly well-designed, making it a top choice for Amazon Fire Stick users. Unlimited simultaneous connections mean you can protect every device in your household without worrying about connection limits.

## Our Verdict

IPVanish earns its position as our Best for Streaming pick. The combination of unlimited devices, SOCKS5 proxy support, optimized streaming servers, and competitive pricing at $2.19/mo makes it ideal for households focused on accessing geo-restricted content across multiple devices.
