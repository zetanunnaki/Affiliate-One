## Overview

Mullvad is unlike any other VPN. There's no account creation — you get a random 16-digit number. No email required. No name. You can pay with cash in an envelope. It's privacy by design at its most radical.

Based in Sweden and charging a flat €5/month with no long-term commitment, Mullvad is built for users who prioritize anonymity above all else.

## What Makes Mullvad Unique

### No Personal Data Required
When you "sign up," Mullvad generates a random account number. That's it. No email address, no name, no password. You log in with just your number. This means Mullvad literally cannot identify you even if compelled by authorities.

### Cash Payments Accepted
You can mail cash (EUR, USD, GBP, SEK, DKK, NOK, CHF) in an envelope to Mullvad's office with your account number. This is the most anonymous payment method available from any VPN provider.

### Flat Pricing, No Tricks
€5/month. No annual discounts, no promotional tricks, no price increases after the first year. The price is the same whether you pay for one month or twelve.

### Real-World Proof
In April 2023, Swedish police executed a search warrant at Mullvad's office. They seized computers but found no customer data — because Mullvad doesn't store any. This was a real-world confirmation of their no-logs policy, not just an audit.

## Limitations

- **Only 5 simultaneous connections** (most competitors offer more)
- **No mobile-first features** like split tunneling on iOS
- **No streaming optimization** — Mullvad doesn't prioritize streaming unblocking
- **No affiliate program** — Mullvad doesn't participate in affiliate marketing (which is why we still review them — editorial independence)
- **Basic apps** — Functional but lack the polish of NordVPN or FastestVPN

## Who Should Use Mullvad

**Best for:** Journalists, activists, whistleblowers, and anyone who needs true anonymity. Also great for privacy-conscious users who want a simple, honest VPN.

**Not ideal for:** Users who want streaming features, users who need more than 5 connections, or those looking for the cheapest option.

**Rating: 4.4/5** — The most honest VPN on the market. What it lacks in features it makes up for in principled design.
