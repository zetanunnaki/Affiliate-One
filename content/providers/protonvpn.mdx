## Overview

Proton VPN comes from the same Swiss team behind ProtonMail, one of the world's most trusted encrypted email services. It's the VPN of choice for journalists, activists, and privacy-focused professionals who want maximum transparency. All apps are open-source, independently audited, and Swiss-based.

## What Sets Proton VPN Apart

### Fully Open-Source
Every Proton VPN app — Windows, macOS, Linux, iOS, Android — is open-source and published on GitHub. Anyone can inspect the code. This is rare among VPN providers and is the gold standard for trustworthiness.

### Swiss Jurisdiction
Switzerland has some of the world's strongest privacy laws. It's outside the EU, not a member of Five Eyes or Fourteen Eyes, and has constitutional protections for privacy. Swiss courts have a high bar for ordering data disclosure.

### Genuine Free Tier
Proton VPN offers a truly free plan with no data caps, no ads, and no speed throttling. Free users get access to servers in the US, Netherlands, and Japan. The free tier is funded by paying subscribers, not by monetizing user data.

### Secure Core
Proton VPN's Secure Core routes your traffic through privacy-friendly countries (Switzerland, Iceland, Sweden) before reaching the exit server. This provides protection even if the exit server is compromised — a threat model that most VPN providers don't address.
