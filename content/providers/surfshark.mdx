## Security

### Encryption & Protocols
AES-256-GCM on OpenVPN, ChaCha20 on WireGuard. Both industry-standard. Surfshark added post-quantum encryption support in late 2025.

### Kill Switch
Tested and functional on all platforms. Blocks traffic immediately on VPN drop. Works reliably on Windows, macOS, iOS, and Android.

### No-Logs Policy
Audited by Deloitte (2023). Surfshark moved from British Virgin Islands to the Netherlands for better infrastructure while maintaining strong privacy. The Netherlands has favorable privacy laws and no mandatory data retention for VPN providers.

### RAM-Only Servers
100% of Surfshark's servers run in RAM-disk mode. No hard drives means no persistent data storage.

## Unique Features

### Unlimited Devices
No connection limit. One subscription covers your laptop, phone, tablet, partner's devices, and your smart TV. No other major VPN offers this.

### CleanWeb
Built-in ad and tracker blocker. Works at the DNS level. In our testing, it blocked 94% of common ad trackers — comparable to a dedicated browser extension.

### MultiHop
Route traffic through two VPN servers in different countries for additional privacy. Adds latency but provides an extra encryption layer for sensitive work.

### Surfshark Nexus
Surfshark's network architecture connects you to the nearest server first, then intelligently routes through their network. This provides IP rotation, MultiHop, and dynamic IP features without the usual speed penalty.
