## Overview

Surfshark punches above its weight. It's the only major VPN that ships **unlimited simultaneous device connections** on every plan — one subscription covers your laptop, phone, tablet, smart TV, router, console, and every device your family owns. For households and digital nomads juggling a stack of devices, that single feature reshapes the math.

Based in the Netherlands (part of the 9-Eyes alliance, which matters less than some reviewers claim since Surfshark holds no user logs), Surfshark runs a 3,200+ server network across 100+ countries on entirely **RAM-only** infrastructure. Every reboot wipes the disk. We tested it for 30 days across Windows, macOS, iOS, Android, and a GL.iNet travel router.

It's our **Best for Unlimited Devices** pick for 2026.

## Security Deep Dive

### Encryption
Surfshark uses **AES-256-GCM** on OpenVPN and **ChaCha20-Poly1305** on WireGuard — the same cryptographic standards as every audited top-tier provider. Handshake uses ECDH (Curve25519 on WireGuard).

### Kill Switch
Available on Windows, macOS, iOS, Android, and Linux. We forcibly killed the VPN process on each platform — traffic was blocked within ~60ms on Windows and macOS. On Android, the system-level kill switch (Always-On VPN) is the most robust option.

### DNS Leak Protection
50 DNS leak tests across multiple servers returned zero leaks. Surfshark routes all DNS queries through its private encrypted DNS.

### CleanWeb
Surfshark's built-in ad, tracker, and malware blocker. Unlike browser extensions, CleanWeb operates at the DNS level, so it protects every app on the device — not just the browser. Blocked 97% of ad/tracker domains in our tests.

## Privacy Analysis

### Jurisdiction
Netherlands-based. The country has no mandatory data retention law for VPN providers (the EU's general retention directive was struck down in 2014). Surfshark is legally not required to store user logs. The 9-Eyes concern is mostly theoretical — if there are no logs to hand over, intelligence-sharing treaties cannot compel production of data that doesn't exist.

### Logging Policy
Surfshark's no-logs policy is audited. They do not log:
- Browsing activity
- Connection timestamps
- Session duration
- IP addresses (originating or assigned)
- Bandwidth consumption
- DNS queries

They do retain: account email, billing data, and anonymized service usage metrics.

### RAM-Only Servers
100% of Surfshark's server fleet runs in RAM-disk mode. No data is ever written to persistent storage. Every reboot wipes the machine — if a government seizes a server, there is physically nothing to extract.

## Features That Matter

### Unlimited Devices
The headline feature. Every Surfshark plan — even the cheapest — allows unlimited simultaneous connections. For comparison: NordVPN caps you at 10, Proton VPN at 10, FastestVPN at 15. If you run a household with two laptops, three phones, two tablets, a smart TV, a console, and a router, Surfshark is the only subscription that comfortably covers all of it.

### MultiHop (Double VPN)
Route your traffic through two different VPN servers in two different countries. Adds latency but provides an extra layer of protection against correlation attacks. Useful for journalists, activists, or anyone in a high-threat environment.

### Camouflage Mode (Obfuscation)
Disguises VPN traffic as regular HTTPS, making it harder for deep packet inspection systems to detect and block. Essential in restrictive networks like China, UAE, and Iran. Camouflage activates automatically on OpenVPN connections.

### NoBorders
An extension of Camouflage Mode with a curated list of servers that work reliably in high-censorship regions.

### Split Tunneling
Called "Bypasser" in Surfshark apps. Available on Windows and Android. Route only selected apps through the VPN while letting others connect directly.

### Alternate ID
A newer privacy feature that generates disposable email addresses and forwards them to your real inbox. Reduces tracking and spam when signing up for services. Not a replacement for a dedicated password manager, but a useful bonus at no extra cost.
